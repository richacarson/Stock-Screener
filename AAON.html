Investment Thesis
AAON is a best-in-class industrial compounder riding the AI data center cooling megatrend. The BASX brand's 138.8% revenue growth and $1.3B backlog provide extraordinary visibility into multi-year demand. Management's willingness to invest through margin compression for long-term capacity positions the company for 18-20% revenue growth with margin expansion to 29-31% in 2026. Minimal debt (D/E 8.6%) and vertically integrated manufacturing create a defensible competitive position.
The primary risk is valuation: at 62x trailing earnings and 27x forward, AAON is priced for flawless execution. Earnings declined 68.6% in 2025 due to facility ramp costs, and any delay in margin recovery could compress the multiple sharply. Data center cooling is becoming a crowded space with well-capitalized competitors, and AAON's 0.50% dividend yield offers minimal income protection. Concentration in data center customers creates cyclical risk if AI capex slows.
