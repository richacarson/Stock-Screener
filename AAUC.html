Investment Thesis
Allied Gold offers leveraged exposure to gold prices with 61.8% revenue growth driven by the gold bull market and production increases. Low debt (D/E 33%) and strong liquidity provide financial resilience. At current gold prices above $3,000/oz, the company's West African assets generate expanding cash flows. The stock could benefit further if gold continues its safe-haven rally amid geopolitical uncertainty.
Key concerns include weak profitability (2.6% ROE despite gold at record highs), concentrated geopolitical risk in Mali and Ivory Coast (political instability, regulatory changes, resource nationalism), and no dividend or earnings visibility (null P/E). Gold mining in West Africa carries ESG concerns around environmental impact and governance. If gold prices retreat, the company's thin margins could quickly turn negative.
