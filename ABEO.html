Investment Thesis
Abeona is at a potential inflection point with pz-cel for RDEB, one of the first gene therapies for a rare genetic skin disease. Trailing P/E of 3.7x and ROE of 70% suggest meaningful revenue recognition, possibly from initial commercialization. The rare disease space offers premium pricing power and orphan drug exclusivity. Faith alignment is strong given the focus on curing children's genetic diseases.
Gene therapy commercialization is extremely challenging—manufacturing complexity, payer coverage, patient identification, and treatment center logistics create significant execution risk. Forward P/E of 18.5x vs trailing 3.7x suggests the market expects earnings normalization lower. The small patient population for RDEB limits total addressable market. Stock price of $4.45 reflects persistent market skepticism about sustainable profitability.
