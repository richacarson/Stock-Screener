Investment Thesis
ABM Industries offers defensive characteristics with its 55-year dividend growth streak, low 20.8% payout ratio, and essential facility services business. The WGNSTAR acquisition positions the company for semiconductor facility growth, while the restructuring program should deliver $35M in annualized savings. FY2026 guidance of $3.85-$4.15 adjusted EPS represents meaningful improvement from trailing results.
The bear case centers on persistent earnings misses — both Q4 FY2025 and Q1 FY2026 fell short of consensus estimates. Revenue growth of 6% masks margin compression in a competitive, labor-intensive industry facing wage inflation. Debt-to-equity of 101% and integration risk from the WGNSTAR acquisition add financial risk. The stock's 15% decline from 50-day SMA suggests the market is skeptical of the earnings recovery timeline.
