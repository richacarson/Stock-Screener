Investment Thesis
Abbott Laboratories is a diversified healthcare titan delivering on a multi-year transformation toward high-growth Medical Devices and Diagnostics. The Medical Devices segment has posted 12 consecutive quarters of double-digit organic growth, FreeStyle Libre is targeting $10B in annual sales by 2028, and the pending Exact Sciences acquisition positions Abbott to lead in cancer diagnostics. Full-year 2025 adjusted EPS of $5.15 grew 10%, with 2026 guidance of $5.55-$5.80 implying continued double-digit earnings growth. The company's AI strategy — from Lingo wearables to AI-guided surgical systems — positions it as a healthcare AI leader rather than a disruption victim.
The bear case centers on segment imbalance and execution risk. Nutrition sales declined 8.9% organically in Q4 2025, the baby formula crisis lingers with DOJ oversight and ongoing lawsuits, and GAAP EPS of $3.72 significantly trails adjusted EPS of $5.15 (suggesting heavy restructuring/litigation charges). The Exact Sciences acquisition adds integration risk to an already complex four-segment business. At a 28x trailing P/E, the stock is priced for execution, and any stumble in Medical Devices growth or acquisition integration could compress the multiple. The 2.4% dividend yield with a 63% payout ratio is safe but not compelling for pure income investors, and the -65 Inspire score creates a headwind for faith-aligned portfolios.
