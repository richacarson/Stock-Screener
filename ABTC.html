Investment Thesis
American Bitcoin offers leveraged exposure to BTC price appreciation through mining operations generating $185M in annual revenue at 50% gross margins, combined with a growing treasury of 6,500+ BTC. The March 2026 capacity expansion to 28.1 EH/s at improved efficiency (13.5 J/TH) should increase mining output. The company's accounting is complex due to FASB fair value BTC adjustments, Hut 8's controlling interest limits minority shareholder influence, and the political association with the Trump family creates ESG and reputational concerns for institutional investors. Energy cost volatility and BTC halving cycles add further uncertainty.
