Investment Thesis
ACADIA offers rare biotech value: a 9x P/E ratio for a company with $1B+ revenue growing 12%, 39.9% ROE, 36.5% profit margins, and $820M in cash. The pipeline targets $11B in combined peak sales potential, with remlifanserin Phase 2 data in Alzheimer's psychosis expected August-October 2026. The 2026 guidance of $1.22-$1.28B revenue confirms accelerating growth. The 2028 target of $1.7B combined net sales (NUPLAZID $1.0B, DAYBUE $0.7B) provides a clear multi-year growth trajectory.
The stock's 14% decline from its 50-day SMA and the wide gap between trailing P/E (9x) and forward P/E (23x) suggest the market questions earnings sustainability. The EMA's negative opinion on trofinetide limits European DAYBUE revenue potential. Remlifanserin Phase 2 data carries binary clinical trial risk. NUPLAZID's growth rate may decelerate as the PDP market matures. Competition from TL1A inhibitors and other neuropsychiatry pipelines could intensify. The forward P/E of 23x, higher than trailing, suggests analysts expect EPS pressure from pipeline investment.
