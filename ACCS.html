Company Profile
Communication Services ·Advertising Agencies ·NYSE American ·United States
ACCESS Newswire Inc. (formerly Issuer Direct Corporation) provides communications technology solutions for public relations and investor relations. Products include press release distribution (ACCESS Newswire), media database and pitching tools, media monitoring, webcasting platform, professional conference/events software, IR content network, and whistleblower hotline. The company rebranded in January 2025 and launched the industry's first pure communications SaaS subscription model. Founded in 1988, headquartered in Raleigh, NC.

AI Resilience (25%)
AI Resilience HIGH RISK
3
AI Risk Protection (2/9): Press release distribution and media outreach — ACCESS Newswire's core business — faces significant AI disruption risk. Large language models can already generate professional press releases, media pitches, and investor relations content at near-zero marginal cost. Companies like Jasper, Copy.ai, and even free ChatGPT are already used by PR professionals to draft releases. The distribution component has some protection (regulatory filing distribution for public companies requires compliance infrastructure), but the value-add of media database, pitching, and monitoring faces AI substitution as AI agents can identify relevant journalists, personalize pitches, and monitor coverage automatically [2]. The company's 91 employees and $22.6M revenue base are small enough that disruption to even one product line is material. Competitors with deeper AI capabilities (e.g., Cision with Brandwatch AI) pose competitive threats. AI Opportunity (1/1): The company has deployed AImee, an AI press release creation tool integrated into the platform, representing an early but real AI revenue contribution through enhanced product value in the subscription model [2].
