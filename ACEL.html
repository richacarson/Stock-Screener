Investment Thesis
Accel delivered record 2025 results with revenue up 8% to $1.33B and net income up 45%, demonstrating strong operating leverage. The forward P/E of 11.8x is attractive for a company with consistent growth and a $900M credit facility providing ample expansion capital. Geographic diversification across ten states reduces Illinois concentration risk, and analyst consensus is Strong Buy with price targets of $13-17.
The gambling industry carries inherent ethical and regulatory risk, with potential for states to restrict or increase taxation on distributed gaming at any time. Debt-to-equity of 225% is elevated, and the company's net debt of $311M requires continued strong cash generation. The CEO transition in August 2026 introduces execution risk, and online gaming growth could gradually cannibalize physical terminal demand.
