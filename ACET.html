Investment Thesis
Adicet's gamma delta T cell platform offers a genuinely differentiated approach to allogeneic cell therapy with potential advantages over conventional CAR-T in manufacturing scalability and safety. Prula-cel's FDA Fast Track designation across three autoimmune indications, rapid enrollment growth (doubling to 20+ patients), and planned Phase 2 pivotal trial initiation in Q2 2026 create multiple near-term catalysts. If successful, the off-the-shelf model could address a multi-billion dollar autoimmune market.
Adicet is a micro-cap ($66M market cap) clinical-stage biotech with zero revenue, deeply negative ROE, and dependence on continued capital raises. Cash runway extends only to H2 2027, creating dilution pressure. The 30% workforce reduction and pipeline discontinuation signal financial stress. Phase 1 data, while promising, requires substantially larger trials to prove efficacy, and the competitive landscape for autoimmune cell therapy is increasingly crowded.
