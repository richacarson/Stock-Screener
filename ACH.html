Investment Thesis
Accendra Health is a deep turnaround play with the core Byram/Apria home health business guided to generate $335-355M in adjusted EBITDA and $100M+ in free cash flow in 2026. At a market cap of just $150M, the stock trades at less than 0.5x guided EBITDA, representing extreme value if management can execute the transformation. The home health market benefits from structural tailwinds including aging demographics and the shift from institutional to home-based care. Analyst targets of $2.75-5.00 suggest 40-155% upside.
The $1.1B loss in 2025 and unknown debt levels post-divestiture create balance sheet uncertainty. Revenue is declining as the divested business rolls off, and the company guided to a weaker first half of 2026 with only 40% of annual EBITDA. The 79% stock decline reflects severe market skepticism about execution risk. With a $1.96 stock price and questionable capital structure, dilution risk through equity raises is significant. The thin analyst coverage and recent transformation make financial projections highly uncertain.
