Investment Thesis
Axcelis is a well-managed semiconductor equipment company trading at 18.7x forward P/E with $557M in cash, minimal debt, and strong free cash flow generation. The Veeco merger creates a combined $4.4B enterprise with $5B+ addressable market and $35M in annual cost synergies. AI-driven demand for HBM and advanced memory is catalyzing a cyclical recovery that benefits ion implantation. The four-quarter EPS beat streak demonstrates execution consistency.
Revenue declined 5.6% and EPS fell 28.3% YoY, reflecting cyclical weakness in semiconductor equipment spending. China revenue dropped from 46% to 32% of sales, and 2026 China demand is expected flat to slightly down. The Q1 2026 guidance of ~$195M suggests continued near-term softness. The Veeco merger introduces integration risk and may not close until H2 2026. Customer digestion of prior capacity investments could extend the downcycle beyond current expectations.
