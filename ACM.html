Investment Thesis
AECOM is a best-in-class infrastructure consulting firm with record $24.83B backlog, 16.8% EBITDA margins, and a clear path to 20%+ margins by FY2028. The dividend has grown 20% annually with a conservative 24.1% payout ratio, and management commits to continued double-digit growth through FY2029. FY2026 guidance of $5.65-5.85 EPS (+9% YoY) with $1.265-1.305B adjusted EBITDA reflects durable growth. AI for Engineering and Oscar LLM position the company as a technology leader in infrastructure. Analyst consensus is Buy with a $142 target (60% upside).
Revenue declined 4.6% YoY and EPS fell 55.3% in FY2025 on an adjusted basis, though adjustments make comparisons complex. Debt-to-equity of 136.6% is elevated for a consulting business. The Construction Management divestiture introduces transition risk and reduces revenue diversification. The 1.29% dividend yield is below the 2% Dividend sleeve threshold, though the exceptional growth rate compensates. Infrastructure spending is cyclical and dependent on government budgets that face fiscal pressure.
