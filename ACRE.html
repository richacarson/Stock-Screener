Company Profile
Real Estate ·REIT - Mortgage ·NYSE ·United States
Ares Commercial Real Estate Corporation is a specialty finance company that originates and invests in commercial real estate loans in the United States. Managed externally by Ares Management, it provides senior mortgage loans, subordinate debt, preferred equity, and mezzanine loans to CRE owners and operators.

Investment Thesis
ACRE benefits from the Ares Management platform, which originated $9B globally in 2025. The company resumed originations with 13 deals totaling $486M in H2 2025, and problem loan resolutions could unlock earnings recovery. The 12% yield provides income while waiting for turnaround, and forward P/E of 7.5x suggests the market prices in recovery.
The 12% yield is a trap: the dividend is not covered by earnings ($0.60 annual vs. -$0.12 distributable EPS) and has been cut 57% over 5 years. Two concentrated problem loans totaling ~$270M create binary risk. Revenue collapsed 82% YoY, ROE is essentially zero, and extreme leverage (D/E 205x) amplifies any credit losses. External management fees further erode returns to common shareholders.
