Investment Thesis
Aclaris has a compelling clinical pipeline led by ATI-2138, which showed 61-77% EASI improvement in Phase 2a atopic dermatitis trials with strong biomarker data. Cash of $151M funds operations through H2 2028, providing runway for multiple clinical catalysts. Four clinical programs in 2026 (ATI-2138, bosakitug, ATI-052, next-gen ITK inhibitor) create multiple shots on goal in the large immunology market.
This is a pre-revenue biotech with a $447M market cap burning $65M annually. Clinical trials are inherently risky, and Phase 2a results in 9 patients require confirmation in larger studies. The immunology space is crowded with well-funded competitors, and even positive data may not be sufficient to differentiate in a market dominated by Dupixent and JAK inhibitors. The stock's significant decline suggests the market is skeptical.
