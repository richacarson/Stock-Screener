Investment Thesis
Ibezapolstat's 96% cure rate with zero recurrences in Phase 2 C. difficile trials is compelling clinical data for an antibiotic addressing a major unmet need. FDA QIDP designation provides 12 years of marketing exclusivity, and patent protection extends to 2039. The $16M market cap provides enormous upside if Phase 3 succeeds and the drug reaches commercialization. The Health~Holland research grant expands the platform's scope.
This is an extreme-risk speculation. The $16M market cap company has only $7.6M in cash, ~10 employees, and a Phase 3 trial not yet initiated (pilot enrollment expected Q4 2026). The 1-for-20 reverse stock split and Bitcoin treasury adoption signal financial distress. Stock moves of 48-218% on news events indicate lottery-ticket volatility. The company will need significant additional capital to fund Phase 3, virtually guaranteeing massive dilution.
