Company Profile
Healthcare ·Biotechnology ·NASDAQ ·China
Adagene is a clinical-stage biotechnology company developing novel antibody-based cancer immunotherapies using its proprietary SAFEbody, NEObody, and POWERbody platforms. Its lead program muzastotug (ADG126) is a masked anti-CTLA-4 antibody in Phase 1b/2 trials for MSS colorectal cancer in combination with pembrolizumab.
