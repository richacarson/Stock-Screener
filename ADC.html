Investment Thesis
Agree Realty is a high-quality net lease REIT offering monthly dividend income with a 3.95% yield, A-minus credit rating, and accelerating AFFO growth. The company's 2026 guidance of 5.4% AFFO per share growth at midpoint, supported by a $1.4-$1.6 billion investment pipeline and over $2 billion in liquidity, provides a solid foundation for continued dividend growth above inflation.
The primary risks include elevated valuation at 37x forward P/E (typical for premium REITs but limiting upside), sensitivity to interest rate movements that could compress cap rate spreads, and concentration in brick-and-mortar retail despite focusing on omni-channel tenants. Revenue growth may decelerate as the portfolio scales and attractive acquisition targets become scarcer.
