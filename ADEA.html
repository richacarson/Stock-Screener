Investment Thesis
Adeia delivered a record 2025 with $443 million in revenue and 72% earnings growth, driven by major licensing wins with Disney, Microsoft, Amazon, and AMD. The company's successful diversification from Pay-TV dependency toward semiconductors and OTT positions it to benefit from AI infrastructure buildout, while the $500 million long-term revenue target provides a clear growth runway at a reasonable 15.5x forward P/E.
The 2026 revenue guidance of $395-$435 million represents a year-over-year decline from the record 2025, largely due to lumpy licensing deal timing and higher litigation expenses. The business model carries inherent concentration risk with large licensing partners, and IP licensing revenue can be volatile based on contract renewal cycles and litigation outcomes. Elevated debt-to-equity of 89% also warrants monitoring.
