Investment Thesis
Analog Devices is a premier semiconductor company with exceptional AI-driven growth tailwinds, delivering $11+ billion in FY2025 revenue with 69.3% gross margins and record $4.3 billion in free cash flow. The company's 20-year dividend growth streak with 10% CAGR, combined with consensus estimates for 24% revenue growth and 40% earnings growth in FY2026, make it a rare combination of quality, growth, and income.
The primary concern is valuation at 56x trailing P/E, though the forward P/E of 24x is more reasonable given growth acceleration. Cyclical semiconductor risk remains, as the current recovery could moderate. US-China trade tensions could disrupt supply chains, and Chinese analog competitors gaining capabilities could pressure margins over time. The 1.65% current yield may not satisfy income-focused investors despite strong growth.
