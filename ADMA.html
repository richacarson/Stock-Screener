Investment Thesis

The yield enhancement technology inflection provides structural margin improvement, while ASCENIV's 51% YoY revenue growth and SG-001 pipeline optionality offer multiple growth vectors at a reasonable 10.4x forward P/E.
The stock has pulled back from $16.39 (50-day SMA) to $14.32, reflecting concerns about the EPS growth deceleration (-52.8% YoY due to prior-period comparisons) and CFO retirement. Concentration risk in IVIG products, dependency on third-party plasma supply, and competition from larger players with broader portfolios remain significant. The lack of dividend means income investors must rely entirely on capital appreciation.
