Investment Thesis
Adient offers deep value potential at 6.1x forward P/E with the world's largest automotive seating market share and an operational turnaround showing momentum. The raised FY2026 guidance ($14.6B revenue, $880M Adj. EBITDA) and Q1 FY2026 earnings beat suggest the turnaround is gaining traction, while the $125M share buyback program signals management confidence.
Adient remains a turnaround story with significant execution risk. GAAP losses persist despite adjusted profitability, D/E of 126% creates financial fragility, and the business is heavily exposed to cyclical auto production volumes. Lower customer production volumes in FY2026 are expected to offset operational improvements, and the competitive pressure from OEMs perpetually compresses seating supplier margins.
