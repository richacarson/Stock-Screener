Investment Thesis
ADT offers deep value at 6.8x forward P/E with a 3.38% dividend yield, backed by the most recognized security brand in America serving 6 million customers. The $170M Origin AI acquisition and $50M technology investment in 2026 position the company for AI-powered smart home evolution, while the long-term framework targets 5% revenue CAGR and 10%+ free cash flow growth through 2030.
ADT's 206% debt-to-equity ratio creates significant financial fragility, and the flat 2026 revenue and EBITDA guidance signals near-term growth stagnation. DIY smart home competitors from Amazon (Ring), Google (Nest), and Apple are disrupting the professional monitoring model with lower-cost AI-enabled alternatives. The zero dividend growth over 5+ years, elevated 13.1% customer attrition, and EBITDA-negative DIY investments all point to a challenging transition period.
