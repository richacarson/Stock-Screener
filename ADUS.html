Investment Thesis
Addus HomeCare is a well-managed home care services company delivering consistent growth with 23.2% revenue growth, 30.3% net income growth, and a 5-year revenue CAGR of 13.2%. The company benefits from powerful secular tailwinds — aging demographics, government preference for home-based care, and Medicaid expansion — at a reasonable 13.3x forward P/E. Strong faith alignment at +45 and conservative 15.8% D/E add quality characteristics.
Revenue growth is expected to decelerate to 7.1% over the next 12 months as the acquisition contribution normalizes, and sell-side estimates may be optimistic. Reimbursement risk from Medicare and Medicaid policy changes remains the primary structural concern, with the Medicaid 80/20 compensation requirement potentially compressing margins. Home health same-store revenue declined 7.54% in Q4, signaling segment weakness. The lack of dividend means income investors must rely on capital appreciation.
