Investment Thesis
ADX offers a compelling combination of 90+ years of dividend reliability, strong 2025 performance beating the S&P 500, and significant AI/tech exposure through its 34.1% Information Technology allocation. The 8% managed distribution rate on NAV, narrowing discount, and experienced management make it an attractive income vehicle for long-term investors seeking broad equity market exposure.
The fund's closed-end structure means shares can trade at persistent discounts to NAV, and the current narrow 3.8% discount leaves less upside from discount compression. Revenue volatility is inherent in a capital-gains-dependent distribution model, and the fund lacks the ability to differentiate meaningfully from low-cost index ETFs on a total-return basis. Rising interest rates could make the yield less competitive relative to fixed income alternatives.
