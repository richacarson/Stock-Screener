Investment Thesis
Addex has a scientifically interesting allosteric modulator platform with potential applications across multiple neurological disorders. The regained Phase 2-ready ADX71149, Neurosterix spin-out advancing M4 PAM for schizophrenia, and Indivior partnership for substance use disorders provide multiple shots on goal at a very low market cap, offering significant upside if any program succeeds.
After 24 years of operation, Addex has failed to bring any product to market or generate meaningful revenue. The $7M market cap and $26K daily trading volume make this stock effectively uninvestable for IOWN. Cash of CHF 2.8M provides extremely limited runway, dilution risk is severe, and the company faces well-funded competitors in every therapeutic area it targets.
