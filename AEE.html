Investment Thesis
Ameren is a premier regulated utility positioned at the nexus of AI infrastructure demand and clean energy transition. With 3 GW of signed data center agreements, a $63B regulated investment pipeline, 13 consecutive years of dividend growth, and 6-8% long-term EPS CAGR guidance, AEE offers rare combination of growth and income in the utility sector. The 2025 EPS beat and reaffirmed 2026 guidance demonstrate execution credibility.
Debt-to-equity of 147% is elevated, reflecting the capital-intensive nature of regulated utility investment. Illinois regulatory challenges — with four ICC orders being appealed — could impair the company's ability to earn its allowed ROE in that territory. Interest charges surged 17% in 2025, and rising rates could compress the utility premium. Revenue declined 8.8% YoY despite earnings growth, suggesting rate mix dependence rather than volume growth.
