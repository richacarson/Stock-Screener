Investment Thesis
AEIS is a premier picks-and-shovels play on AI infrastructure, with Data Center Computing revenue doubling to $587M in 2025 and guided for 30%+ growth in 2026. The company's precision power solutions are essential for hyperscaler AI racks, and new semiconductor products for sub-2nm nodes position it for market share gains. With revenue growing 21% to $1.8B, margins at 5-year highs, record cash flow, and $2.5B+ revenue-capable infrastructure, AEIS has the platform to deliver sustained high-teens growth.
The trailing P/E of 82x prices in significant future growth, creating vulnerability if AI infrastructure spending decelerates. Forward P/E of 29.8x is more reasonable but still premium. Tariff headwinds from the new Thailand factory and global supply chain could pressure margins. Semiconductor revenue grew only 6% YoY, and a longer-than-expected downturn in chip capex could offset data center gains. The company's rapid growth in data center is partially from a low base, and sustaining 30%+ growth will be increasingly challenging as the business scales.
