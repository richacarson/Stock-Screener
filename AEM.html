Investment Thesis
Agnico Eagle is the premier senior gold miner, delivering record free cash flow of $4.4B in 2025 with AISC of $1,339/oz providing massive margins at current gold prices above $3,000/oz. The near-zero leverage (1.3% D/E), $2.9B net cash position, and 37.5% profit margin create a fortress balance sheet, while the 13.0x forward P/E significantly undervalues a company with record reserves of 55.4M ounces and a pipeline targeting 4M+ ounces annually by the early 2030s. The 12.5% dividend increase and $600M share buyback program demonstrate shareholder commitment alongside the record $565-$635M exploration investment.
The bear case recognizes Agnico Eagle's complete dependence on gold prices—the 60.3% revenue growth and 134% EPS growth in 2025 were primarily driven by record gold prices rather than operational transformation. If gold prices revert toward $2,000/oz, earnings would compress dramatically even with stable production. The 2026 AISC guidance of $1,400-$1,550/oz (12% increase) signals cost inflation pressure, with 40% reflecting genuine cost escalation. The Whale Tail caribou controversy and federal investigation into compliance create regulatory and social license risks in Nunavut, a jurisdiction critical to Agnico's growth pipeline. The Inspire MIXED score (0) provides no faith-based differentiation.
