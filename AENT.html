Investment Thesis
Alliance Entertainment is the dominant U.S. physical media distributor riding a vinyl and collectibles renaissance, with exclusive distribution agreements with Amazon MGM and Paramount Pictures securing premium content. H1 FY2026 showed gross margin expansion of 260bps and net income nearly doubling, demonstrating the shift to higher-value products is working. Forward P/E of 9.7x and ROE of 20.5% represent attractive value for a market leader with improving profitability. The analyst target of $10 implies 52% upside.
Physical media distribution is a structurally challenged industry facing long-term decline from digital streaming. Revenue declined 6.3% YoY, and the Q2 revenue drop of $25M was partially masked by margin improvement. Debt-to-equity of 91.2% is concerning for a company in a declining market. Daily dollar volume of $527K is borderline illiquid. The company's dependence on a few major studio relationships creates concentration risk — loss of the Amazon MGM or Paramount deals would be devastating. The $34M arcade revenue slump shows vulnerability to segment-specific downturns.
