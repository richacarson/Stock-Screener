Revenue doubled in 2025 with 70-100% growth guided for 2026, and marquee partnerships with NVIDIA, LG Innotek, a top 5 global OEM, and Forterra validate commercial demand. If key partnerships like NVIDIA DRIVE Hyperion do not generate volume orders, the equity could be severely impaired.
