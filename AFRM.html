Investment Thesis
Affirm is the leading US BNPL platform with 29% revenue growth, sustained GAAP profitability, and forward P/E of just 11.5x on twice-raised FY2026 guidance. The AI agentic commerce partnership with Stripe positions Affirm at the frontier of how consumers will transact in an AI-native world. 478,000 merchants, 26M users, and partnerships with Amazon, Shopify, Walmart, Lowe's, and Wayfair create a powerful network effect. Analyst consensus is Buy with $83.65 target (91% upside).
The stock has declined 40% from 2025 highs to $43.81, reflecting LENDX private-credit liquidity concerns and broader fintech selloff. Trailing P/E of 54x remains expensive, D/E of 258.9% creates significant credit cycle risk, and net margin of 7.6% is thin for the valuation premium. BNPL faces ongoing regulatory scrutiny from the CFPB, and a recession would increase default rates across Affirm's consumer loan portfolio. Competition from Klarna, PayPal, and traditional credit card issuers intensifies, and consumer credit quality could deteriorate in an economic slowdown.
