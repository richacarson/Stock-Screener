Investment Thesis
AGCO enters 2026 at the bottom of the agricultural cycle with record free cash flow, growing market share, and a leading precision agriculture technology stack. The company's 2026 guidance of $10.4-$10.7B in revenue with EPS of $5.50-$6.00 reflects expected cyclical recovery, while AI-driven autonomous farming solutions position AGCO for long-term margin expansion as adoption scales.
The agricultural equipment industry is cyclical, and tariff uncertainty could dampen the recovery. AGCO's reliance on global agricultural spending exposes it to commodity price volatility, weather events, and trade policy shifts. The stock has underperformed its 50-day SMA, suggesting near-term market skepticism about the pace of recovery.
