Agenus Inc. is a clinical-stage immuno-oncology company developing next-generation immunotherapy combinations, including botensilimab (CTLA-4) and balstilimab (PD-1). The company's lead program targets microsatellite stable colorectal cancer with Phase 3 trials underway.

Excellence Evaluation — Think Like an Owner
Innovation STRONG
7
Agenus has developed a differentiated Fc-enhanced CTLA-4 antibody (botensilimab) that shows meaningful activity in MSS colorectal cancer, a notoriously immunotherapy-resistant indication. The BOT+BAL combination achieved a 42% two-year overall survival rate in refractory MSS mCRC and 71% ORR when combined with chemotherapy. Over 1,200 patients treated across clinical trials.

Investment Thesis
Agenus's BOT+BAL combination shows differentiated clinical activity in MSS colorectal cancer, an area of significant unmet need with no approved immunotherapy options. Phase 3 BATTMAN trial enrollment and expanding compassionate access in France validate the clinical promise. The $141M Zydus deal provides near-term financial runway.
Agenus carries substantial binary risk as a clinical-stage biotech with minimal revenue and a $142M market cap. Phase 3 trial failure would be catastrophic, and the company may need additional dilutive financing. The stock's extreme volatility and low price point reflect speculative risk that may not suit most investment frameworks.
