Investment Thesis
Alamos Gold is executing one of the strongest growth trajectories in the gold sector, targeting 83% production growth to 1M ounces annually. Record free cash flow, a fortress balance sheet with just $200M debt and $623M cash, and 32% reserve growth create a compelling setup. The Phase 3+ Expansion completion in H2 2026 is a near-term catalyst for production acceleration.
Gold price is the dominant risk — the company's economics are modeled at $4,500/oz, and any significant gold price correction would pressure returns and valuations. Production in 2025 missed guidance due to weather, highlighting operational risk. The stock's forward P/E of 12x already prices in significant growth, and remaining legacy hedges at $1,821/oz create near-term margin drag.
