Company Profile
Real Estate ·REIT - Mortgage ·NASDAQ ·United States
AGNC Investment Corp. is an internally managed mortgage REIT that invests primarily in Agency residential mortgage-backed securities (RMBS) guaranteed by Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, or Ginnie Mae. Founded in 2008, the company has paid over $15 billion in dividends since inception and maintains a monthly dividend payment schedule.

Investment Thesis
AGNC Investment offers one of the highest yields in public equity markets at 13.4% through monthly $0.12 dividends, backed by Agency MBS investments with credit guarantees from Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, and Ginnie Mae — eliminating default risk on the underlying securities [1]. The company has paid $15B+ in cumulative dividends since inception across 16 consecutive years. At Forward P/E of 7.1x with Q1 2026 core EPS of $0.42 comfortably covering the $0.12 monthly dividend, income coverage remains adequate despite a GAAP net loss [1]. CEO Federico's expansion to CIO role and the new data analytics initiative signal operational investment [2]. AGNC's fourth annual ESG report demonstrates institutional-quality governance for a 54-person organization [5].
The bear case centers on structural fragility. D/E of 723% means any sustained interest rate increase or MBS spread widening could compress book value and threaten the dividend [6]. The payout ratio of 112.5% indicates the current dividend exceeds earnings, and the -3% five-year dividend CAGR with zero consecutive years of growth confirms a history of dividend cuts [4]. Q1 2026 produced a GAAP net loss of $148M, and the company projects $2.3B revenue by 2028 requiring 32.3% annual growth — aggressive for a mature mortgage REIT [1]. The PEG ratio of 3.48 suggests the stock is overvalued relative to growth. AGNC's returns depend entirely on the interest rate environment, mortgage prepayment speeds, and Fed policy — factors entirely outside management's control [6].
