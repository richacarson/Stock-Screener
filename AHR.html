Investment Thesis
AHR is riding the powerful demographic wave of America's aging population with a diversified portfolio of 319 healthcare properties delivering 14.2% same-store NOI growth. The SHOP segment is driving double-digit NFFO per share growth with 2026 guidance pointing to continued momentum. Analyst upgrades from Jefferies and Scotiabank validate the thesis.
The stock trades at a lofty 114x GAAP P/E (though FFO-based metrics are more relevant for REITs), and the D/E ratio of 50x is elevated. The leadership transition with an interim CEO introduces execution risk, and the $1.1B in equity issuance in 2025 diluted existing shareholders. Integration of acquired properties could stumble if occupancy trends weaken.
