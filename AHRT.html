Investment Thesis
AHRT is executing a bold turnaround under new leadership, selling $562M in multifamily assets to deleverage and refocus on high-performing retail and office properties with 95.3% occupancy and positive renewal spreads. If the restructuring succeeds, the current $5.71 share price could represent significant upside from a leaner, more focused REIT.
The D/E ratio of 199x is dangerously high, and revenue has plummeted 47% due to asset sales. The dividend was already cut and remains at risk with a 94% payout ratio. The rebranding and restructuring introduce significant execution risk, and the commercial real estate market faces headwinds from remote work trends and higher interest rates.
