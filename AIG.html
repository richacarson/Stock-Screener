Investment Thesis
AIG is executing a successful multi-year transformation through AIG Next, delivering 56% EPS growth, 13.6% Core Operating ROE, and $6.8B in shareholder returns. At a forward P/E of 8.4x, the stock is attractively valued relative to improving fundamentals. AI adoption through Anthropic and Palantir partnerships positions AIG as a tech-forward insurer.
Revenue declined 7.2% as the company optimizes its portfolio, and the stock has fallen 13% since the start of 2026. D/E of 24.4x is typical for insurers but represents significant leverage. The insurance industry faces catastrophe risk from climate change, and AIG's history of near-collapse in 2008 remains a governance overhang. Corebridge separation execution remains a key risk.
