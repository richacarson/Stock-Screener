Investment Thesis
AII is a Florida property insurer delivering exceptional financial metrics: 3.3x P/E, 42.1% ROE, 63.7% combined ratio, and 30% gross premium growth to $885M. The $100M IPO proceeds fund expansion into new states while policies-in-force grew 48.6% YoY. Favorable reinsurance market conditions with 10-20% rate decreases expected in 2026 should improve margins further.
Florida concentration (93.7% of policies) creates catastrophic hurricane risk that could devastate the book in a single event. The company has limited public market history since its May 2025 IPO. Daily trading volume of $2.6M provides adequate liquidity but limits institutional interest. The special dividend signals confidence but also uses capital that could buffer against catastrophe losses.
