Investment Thesis
AIV offers a potential liquidation arbitrage with estimated total distributions of $5.75-$7.10 per share versus a current stock price of $4.05, representing 42-75% potential upside. The company has $394.9M cash and contracts to sell 12 properties for ~$680M with $30M non-refundable deposits. The $1.45 initial liquidating distribution was paid in March 2026 with additional Q2 distributions of $0.85-0.95 expected.
Liquidation estimates are inherently uncertain — actual sale prices may differ from expectations, particularly if real estate markets weaken. The timeline for full liquidation is unclear, reducing IRR. Debt of 157% of equity must be retired before equity distributions. Development and land assets may sell below expectations. Tax treatment of liquidating distributions adds complexity. This is a special situation investment, not a growth or income play.
