Company Profile
Technology ·AI Software & Consumer Health Technology ·NASDAQ ·United States
Avalon GloboCare Corp. is a technology-innovation company operating through two segments: Consumer Health Technology and AI Content Technology. The Consumer Health Technology segment develops KetoAir, an FDA Class I breath-based metabolic monitoring device. The AI Content Technology segment operates through RPM Interactive Inc., which offers the Catch-Up generative AI video platform. The company is also developing Avalon Quantum AI agentic AI systems. Formerly focused on cell therapy and real estate, the company has pivoted to AI and consumer health products. Headquartered in the United States with operations in China.
25 Employees ·www.avalon-globocare.com
