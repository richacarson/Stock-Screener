Investment Thesis
Allegiant is poised for an earnings inflection in 2026 with guided EPS above $8 (vs. GAAP loss in 2025), driven by 737 MAX fuel savings, Sun Country synergies, and CASM improvement. Forward P/E of 7.5x on $8+ EPS guidance with leverage declining to 2.3x makes the risk/reward compelling for a recovery play.
The bear case includes high leverage at 177% D/E, GAAP unprofitability in 2025, execution risk on Sun Country integration, and airline industry cyclicality. The $750M CapEx budget in 2026 strains free cash flow. Macro uncertainty around consumer travel spending could undermine the recovery thesis.
