Investment Thesis
Alliance Laundry is the dominant global player in commercial laundry with 40% North American share, 25.5% EBITDA margins, and 13% revenue growth. The essential nature of laundry services provides recession resistance. 2026 guidance of 5-7% revenue growth with deleveraging to low 2x range should drive earnings expansion as interest expense declines. Net income margin of only 6% despite 25.5% EBITDA highlights heavy debt service. The stock has declined from IPO price of $22 to $19.34, and lock-up expirations could create selling pressure.
