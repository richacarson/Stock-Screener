Investment Thesis
Alkami is a high-growth SaaS platform ($444M revenue +33%, $480M ARR) serving the large community banking digitization opportunity with $1.7B in remaining performance obligations. 2026 guidance of $525-531M revenue and $94-98M adjusted EBITDA shows improving unit economics. Forward P/E of 15.2x for a 33% grower approaching profitability is attractive following the 50% stock decline.
The bear case includes continued GAAP losses, D/E of 102%, the 50% stock decline suggesting institutional selling, and competition from well-resourced legacy providers (Fiserv, FIS, Jack Henry) and newer entrants. Community banking consolidation could reduce the addressable market. The stock's underperformance despite strong fundamentals may indicate the market questions the long-term margin profile.
