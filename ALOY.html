Investment Thesis
REalloys addresses a critical U.S. national security gap — domestic rare earth supply chain independence. The 2027 U.S. defense procurement ban on Chinese rare earth sourcing creates a regulatory tailwind. Patent-pending HF-free processing and the largest heavy rare earth metallization facility outside China are meaningful differentiators.
Extreme speculative risk with $690M market cap on only $2.4M TTM revenue. Production doesn't begin until 2027, creating a long execution runway with capital needs. The stock merged via SPAC (Blackboxstocks) in Feb 2026, and SPAC-originated companies have a poor track record. Rare earth prices are volatile and dependent on Chinese export policies.
