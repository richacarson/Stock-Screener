Company Profile

Antero Midstream owns, operates, and develops midstream energy assets in the Appalachian Basin. The company provides gathering, compression, processing, and water handling services through its Marcellus and Utica Shale operations, serving as the critical first link to global LNG and LPG export markets.

Investment Thesis
Antero Midstream offers a stable midstream infrastructure play with eleven consecutive years of EBITDA growth, strong 2026 guidance ($1.2B+ EBITDA, $360M FCF after dividends), and a 4% dividend yield. The $1.1B HG Midstream acquisition adds 400+ undeveloped locations and water asset growth potential. The company's low capital intensity and 20% ROIC create a cash generation engine with balanced shareholder returns through dividends and buybacks.
Risks include high debt-to-equity of 163%, a dividend payout ratio exceeding 100% on an earnings basis (though cash-covered), and customer concentration with Antero Resources. The flat $0.90/share dividend offers no growth premium, and the P/E of 26x is elevated for a midstream company. Fossil fuel exposure creates long-term secular risk as energy transition accelerates.
