Investment Thesis
Applied Materials is the indispensable picks-and-shovels play for the AI revolution, with its equipment required for manufacturing every leading-edge AI chip. The company achieved 25-year-high gross margins, generated $5.7B in FCF, and expects 20%+ semiconductor systems growth in calendar 2026.
