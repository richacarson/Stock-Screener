Investment Thesis

BAQSIMI revenue grew 12% to $185M and could reach $250-275M at peak. The ipratropium bromide HFA launch in Q2 2026 provides near-term catalyst.
Legacy product declines are accelerating with glucagon down 45% and epinephrine down 9% due to generic competition. Q4 2025 earnings missed consensus by 21.5%, and gross margins are expected to decline in 2026 due to pricing pressure. The company's pipeline is promising but unproven in proprietary drugs, and the strategic transition will take several years.
