Investment Thesis
The pending MasterBrand merger offers a potential floor for the stock, with the combined company targeting $639M pro forma adjusted EBITDA and $90M in cost synergies. The stock has already fallen significantly (from $54.58 50-day SMA) and a housing recovery would benefit the combined entity significantly. The merger received shareholder approval from both companies in October 2025.
Revenue declines of 12-18% across quarters, EBITDA margin compression from 12.3% to 9.2%, and a $30.1M goodwill impairment signal deteriorating fundamentals. The housing market remains weak with both new construction and remodel softening. Tariff impacts of 4-4.5% of annualized sales add further pressure. ROE of just 1.9% reflects value destruction, and merger completion risk remains until the deal closes.
