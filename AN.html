Investment Thesis
AutoNation offers compelling value at a forward P/E of 7.6x with exceptional cash generation ($1B+ free cash flow), a 27.1% ROE, and a high-margin after-sales business (48.7% margin). The company has demonstrated disciplined capital allocation with $1.5B deployed in 2025 and a 10% share reduction. AN Finance's turnaround to profitability adds a new earnings lever. The stock is down 10% from its 50-day SMA, creating an entry opportunity.
Revenue declined 3.9% in Q4 and management expects the market to be 'slightly down in 2026,' suggesting continued volume pressure. Debt-to-equity of 436.9% is extremely elevated, creating vulnerability in a downturn. The 60% drop in EV volumes highlights the difficulty of navigating the powertrain transition. Tariff impacts on vehicle prices could suppress demand further, and cyclical exposure to consumer spending creates downside risk in a recession.
