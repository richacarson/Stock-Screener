Company Profile
Healthcare ·Biotechnology ·NASDAQ ·United States
AnaptysBio is a clinical-stage biotechnology company developing immunology therapeutics and holding valuable royalty assets from Jemperli (GSK) and imsidolimab (Vanda). The company plans to spin off its biopharma operations into First Tracks Biotherapeutics (TRAX) by mid-2026 while retaining royalty assets.

Investment Thesis
AnaptysBio's Jemperli royalty stream ($1.4B annualized sales, projecting $390M+ in peak royalties) provides a valuable income floor, while the pipeline (rosnilimab in RA, ANB033 in celiac, ANB101 as potential best-in-class BDCA2) offers upside optionality. Guggenheim's $90 price target implies 44% upside. The Q2 2026 spin-off could unlock value by separating the predictable royalty stream from the higher-risk pipeline. Cash of $311.6M provides ample runway.
The rosnilimab UC failure demonstrates clinical risk, and the RA program advancement depends on 'strategic or other outside sources of capital.' The $600M non-recourse debt against the royalty stream limits the upside from Jemperli. The pending GSK/Tesaro litigation (trial July 2026) creates binary outcome risk. Negative forward P/E and high debt-to-equity reflect a company that is not yet self-sustaining from operations.
