Investment Thesis
Angi's bull case rests on the completion of the homeowner choice transition: proprietary channels grew 23% in Q4 2025 and 17% for the full year, suggesting the new marketplace model is gaining traction even as network channels decline [1]. At a Forward P/E of 5.1x and $298M market cap, the stock prices in extreme pessimism; AI-driven cost savings of $70-80M annually from the 350-person reduction represent a significant margin uplift for a company generating $1.03B in revenue [3]. If revenue growth returns to positive territory in H2 2026 as guided, the re-rating potential is substantial.
The bear case is that Angi is a declining business facing structural disintermediation. Google Local Services Ads and AI-powered voice assistants (Google Assistant, Alexa, Siri) threaten to bypass Angi's marketplace entirely, connecting consumers directly with professionals [3]. FY2025 revenue declined 13% to $1.03B, and 2026 guidance for only 1-3% growth suggests the best case is modest stabilization, not meaningful recovery [1]. The extreme PEG ratio of 117.6 indicates minimal earnings growth expectations, and the 12% workforce reduction and executive team rated bottom 5% on Comparably signal organizational dysfunction. IAC's controlling ownership limits governance independence and potential for strategic alternatives.
