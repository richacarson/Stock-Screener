Investment Thesis
Adlai Nortye offers a diversified oncology pipeline with six candidates spanning novel modalities, highlighted by the potentially first-in-class Pan-RAS(ON) inhibitor AN9025 (first patient dosed Feb 2026) and the AN4035 ADC (73% preclinical ORR). The $140M PIPE and ASK Pharma licensing deal provide financial runway and pipeline validation.
High-risk clinical-stage biotech with no revenue, extremely elevated D/E of 562.6, and a Phase 3 failure already on record (BURAN). The $140M PIPE represents massive dilution, and dual U.S.-China operations create regulatory and geopolitical complexity. Multiple binary clinical readouts ahead create significant volatility risk.
