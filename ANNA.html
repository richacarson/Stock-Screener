Investment Thesis
AleAnna offers rare exposure to Italian natural gas at a time when EU energy security is a top priority following Russian gas phase-out. Longanesi field is generating strong cash flow at $13.50/Mcf Italian gas prices, proved reserves jumped 47% in 2025, and the Gradizza field (100% owned) targets first production in Q1 2027. The RNG portfolio provides long-term growth optionality.
Extreme stock volatility (91% single-day gain in March 2026) suggests speculative trading dynamics. The company has an analyst price target of just $2.50 despite trading at $7.07, implying significant downside risk. Still transitioning from cash-burning explorer to profitable producer, with ROE at -11.5%. Heavy dependency on volatile Italian natural gas prices.
