Investment Thesis
StoneBridge Acquisition II is a blank check SPAC targeting APAC/EMEA businesses with an April 2027 deadline. The trust value near $10 provides downside protection for patient investors who can redeem shares if no deal materializes.
The management team's previous SPAC resulted in a -99% loss for investors who held through the DigiAsia combination, creating a serious credibility deficit. With no identified target and extremely low liquidity (~$119K daily volume), this is a highly speculative vehicle unsuitable for most investors.
