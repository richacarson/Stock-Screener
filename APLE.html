Investment Thesis
Apple Hospitality REIT offers an 8.28% monthly dividend yield from a high-quality portfolio of 220 Marriott and Hilton-branded hotels across 85 U.S. markets. The P/AFFO of 10.84x is reasonable for a lodging REIT, and 59% of hotels face no nearby construction, providing pricing protection. Manageable leverage of 3.4x EBITDA with well-staggered maturities and $587M available on the revolver support the dividend.
RevPAR declined 1.6% in 2025 with flat guidance for 2026, suggesting a hotel cycle that has peaked. The 8.28% yield comes with no dividend growth (0% five-year CAGR), meaning total return depends entirely on yield plus any capital appreciation. Government and weekday business demand weakness, combined with challenging comps, create near-term headwinds. The COVID dividend cut demonstrates that the payout is not inviolable.
