Investment Thesis
Arm is the foundational architecture of the AI revolution, powering virtually every major AI chip and benefiting from secular tailwinds in data center, edge AI, automotive, and robotics. With four consecutive billion-dollar quarters, 26% revenue growth, and the deepest semiconductor IP ecosystem on the planet, Arm is positioned to compound royalty revenue for decades as compute proliferates.
The bear case centers on extreme valuation — 176x trailing P/E prices in flawless execution for years. Margin compression from chip manufacturing ambitions, competition from open-source RISC-V, and SoftBank's controlling influence create risks. A cyclical semiconductor downturn could hit sentiment hard at these multiples.
