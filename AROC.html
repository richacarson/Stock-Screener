Investment Thesis
Archrock is the dominant natural gas compression services provider benefiting from a structural upcycle driven by LNG export expansion and AI data center power demand. Record 2025 revenue of $1.49B, 95% EPS growth, five consecutive dividend increases, and a forward P/E of 15.2 make it attractively valued for a company with strong visibility into multi-year demand growth.
The bear case includes high leverage (163% D/E) from capital-intensive acquisitions, cyclical exposure to natural gas prices, and long-term energy transition risk as renewables and electrification reduce gas demand. ESG-conscious investors may avoid fossil fuel infrastructure companies regardless of financial performance.
