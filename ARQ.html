Company Profile

Arq, Inc. produces and sells powdered activated carbon (PAC) and granular activated carbon (GAC) products used for mercury removal and water treatment at coal-fired power plants and industrial facilities. The company reported record PAC volumes of 117M+ pounds in 2025 while pausing its GAC production for engineering review.

Investment Thesis
Arq's core PAC business is stable with record volumes, 96% contract visibility for 2026, and improving ASPs from $0.82 to $0.89. The 2026 EBITDA guidance of $17-20M represents 35-50% improvement over 2025, suggesting the core business can support the valuation as the GAC distraction is removed. The bear case is severe: a $52.6M net loss, $44.8M impairment, collapsing stock price (-39% from SMA50), leadership changes, and complete abandonment of the GAC growth strategy that was the company's primary differentiation narrative. The core PAC business serves a declining market as coal-fired power plants retire. Without GAC, Arq is a shrinking addressable market company trading at 23x forward earnings.
