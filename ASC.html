Investment Thesis
Ardmore benefits from favorable tanker market dynamics: 16% of the global fleet under sanctions, an aging MR fleet, and LR2s shifting to crude trades. Low 17% leverage, $11,700/day breakeven, and limited 2026-2027 dockings support earnings power. Q1 2026 MR rates of $29,100/day are improving.
However, tanker earnings are inherently volatile and the P/E of 16.6 prices in optimistic rate assumptions. The variable dividend provides no yield floor. FY2025 earnings fell 68% from 2024, demonstrating the cyclical nature. A downturn in global oil trade or increased tonnage supply could compress earnings sharply.
