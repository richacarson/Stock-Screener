Investment Thesis
Ategrity is a high-quality specialty insurer growing GWP at 28% CAGR with an 88.2% combined ratio, conservative 0.3x D/E, and A.M. Best 'A-' rating. The proprietary underwriting platform and focus on underserved E&S SMBs provide a durable competitive moat. Forward P/E of 7.5 is attractive for a company growing premiums 30%+.
However, ASIC is a recent IPO (June 2025) with limited public track record. Zimmer Financial still owns a majority stake, creating overhang risk. The E&S market is cyclical and could see rate softening as capital flows in. Daily liquidity of ~$2.2M is thin for institutional investors. IPO lockup and sponsor dynamics add uncertainty.
