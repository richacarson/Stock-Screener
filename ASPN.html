Company Profile
Industrials ·Building Products & Equipment ·NYSE ·United States
Aspen Aerogels designs, develops, and manufactures aerogel-based thermal management solutions. Its PyroThin thermal barriers protect EV batteries from thermal runaway, while its energy industrial products insulate LNG, subsea, and refinery infrastructure. General Motors is its dominant customer, representing 59% of 2025 revenue.
