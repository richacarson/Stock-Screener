Investment Thesis
Strive offers leveraged exposure to Bitcoin (~10,900 BTC at ~$1.26B) plus a growing anti-ESG ETF business ($2B+ AUM). If Bitcoin appreciates and the anti-ESG movement gains traction under a sympathetic administration, the stock could see significant upside. The Semler Scientific acquisition adds Bitcoin holdings.
The stock is essentially a leveraged Bitcoin proxy with extreme volatility, massive dilution risk (1.3B shares filed), and a politically polarizing brand that limits its addressable market. The company has negative earnings, founder leadership vacuum, and no clear path to operational profitability independent of Bitcoin price movements. The anti-ESG positioning explicitly opposes environmental and social stewardship.
