Company Profile
Technology ·Scientific & Technical Instruments ·NASDAQ ·United States
Astrotech Corporation develops and commercializes mass spectrometry-based detection and analysis systems. Its subsidiaries include 1st Detect (explosive/narcotic trace detection), AgLAB (agriculture), Pro-Control (industrial process), BreathTech (medical diagnostics), and EN-SCAN (environmental testing). The flagship TRACER 1000 is deployed in 35 locations across 16 countries.
