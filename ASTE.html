Company Profile

Astec Industries designs, manufactures, and sells equipment and components for road building, aggregate processing, and materials handling. Operating through Infrastructure Solutions and Materials Solutions segments, the company serves construction contractors and mining operations. Founded in 1972 and headquartered in Chattanooga, TN.

Investment Thesis
Astec delivered record Q4 sales, a 22.5% backlog increase to $514.1M, and guided to $170-190M adjusted EBITDA for 2026. Federal infrastructure funding (IIJA) provides a multi-year demand tailwind. The forward P/E of 11.3x is attractive for a company with improving margins, growing backlog, and $314.7M liquidity. Materials Solutions growth of 60.6% signals share gains.
Trailing P/E of 29.7x reflects the cyclical earnings trough rather than normalized profitability. EPS declined 43.8% YoY despite revenue growth, raising margin concerns. The 0.8% dividend yield provides negligible income. Construction equipment is inherently cyclical — IIJA tailwinds will eventually fade. ROE of 5.9% is below cost of capital for most investors.
