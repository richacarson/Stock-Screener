Company Profile
Technology ·Software - Infrastructure ·NASDAQ ·United States
authID Inc. provides a cloud-based biometric identity verification and authentication platform. The company's solutions include facial biometric authentication, identity proofing, and passwordless login for enterprises. Founded in 2009, headquartered in Denver, Colorado, authID serves customers in financial services, healthcare, and retail with its IDX and PrivacyKey platforms.

Investment Thesis
authID operates in the structurally growing identity verification market, which benefits from increasing AI-generated fraud, deepfake threats, and enterprise zero-trust adoption. The PrivacyKey quantum-resistant platform and Agentic AI identity binding represent forward-looking product innovation, and Q1 2026 revenue grew 62% YoY to $0.5M with ARR reaching $1.9M [1]. The $40M enterprise pipeline and Microsoft ecosystem partnerships suggest commercial traction is building, and the company's focus on biometric identity positions it as an indirect beneficiary of AI proliferation.
However, authID is a micro-cap ($17.8M market cap) pre-profitability company burning $3.4M/quarter with only $4.2M in bridge financing to extend its runway [1]. The stock is deeply illiquid ($131K daily dollar volume), triggering the IOWN liquidity floor and making it unsuitable for institutional investors. Revenue of $2M TTM against $18M in annual operating expenses means the company needs a 9x revenue increase just to break even. Competition from Okta ($34B market cap), Ping Identity, and native platform integrations from Microsoft and Google represents existential threats to a 46-employee company. The risk of equity dilution or failure to secure additional financing makes this a speculative venture bet, not a portfolio-grade holding.
