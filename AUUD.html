Company Profile
Technology ·Software - Application ·NASDAQ ·United States
Auddia is a Boulder, Colorado-based micro-cap developing an AI platform for audio. Its flagship faidr app removes commercials from AM/FM streams and inserts personalized content, and the company launched Discovr Radio in January 2026 as a B2B artist/label promotion platform. Auddia is pursuing a proposed business combination with Thramann Holdings to form McCarthy Finney, an AI-native holding company.

Investment Thesis
Auddia is a ~$2.3M market cap AI audio micro-cap with a newly launched Discovr Radio B2B platform targeting artists and labels who pay to have songs placed into AM/FM streaming ad slots [1]. The platform went live January 20, 2026 with ~300 initial customers and expects first B2B revenue in Q1 2026 [1]. The company is pivoting from consumer-app economics (faidr, now free) to a B2B subscription model and has proposed a merger with Thramann Holdings to become McCarthy Finney, an AI-native holding company [2][3]. The 46% stock pop on February 18, 2026 following the Discovr launch reflects speculative interest in the AI audio pivot. The thesis, if it worked, would be narrow but meaningful: route a sliver of the global music promotion budget through an AI placement engine.
The bear case is overwhelming. Auditors raised substantial doubt about going-concern status; 2025 net loss was $7.7M on $5.6M operating cash burn with only $3.2M of year-end cash and funding runway to Q2 2026 [3]. Auddia has executed back-to-back reverse stock splits (1-for-17 in March 2025, 1-for-7.7 on April 1, 2026) and is seeking authority for yet another reverse split at a ratio up to 1-for-200 at the May 8, 2026 special meeting — a textbook distress pattern [3]. The proposed Thramann Holdings merger would leave legacy Auddia shareholders with only ~20% of the combined entity, subject to a $12M minimum cash condition [3]. Liquidity is thin at $1.39M daily dollar volume. Competing in audio against Spotify, Apple, Amazon, and SiriusXM, Auddia has no defensible moat at this scale. SELL.
