Investment Thesis
Axon is the dominant AI-powered public safety platform with 90%+ TASER market share, 60-80% body camera share, and rapidly growing software ARR of $1.3B. The company's AI products (Draft One, Axon Assistant) are driving unprecedented upselling and 125% net revenue retention. With $14.4B in contracted backlog and 27-30% revenue growth guidance for 2026, Axon has exceptional visibility.
The primary risk is valuation: at 333x trailing P/E and 47x forward P/E, the stock prices in substantial growth. Any slowdown in AI adoption or government procurement cycles could compress the multiple significantly. Dependence on government funding, potential regulatory pushback on AI in policing, and the stock's 18% decline from its August 2025 all-time high suggest near-term volatility.
