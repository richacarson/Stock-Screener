Investment Thesis
AXT is positioned at the intersection of two mega-trends: AI infrastructure buildout and silicon photonics. InP substrates are essential for high-speed data center interconnects, and demand is surging. The company raised $100M to double InP capacity in 2026 and is broadening its Tier-1 customer base. The Tongmei STAR Market IPO provides additional value creation potential.
Significant risks include ongoing net losses ($21.3M in 2025), extreme forward P/E of 116x, and critical dependence on Chinese export permits for InP shipments. Revenue declined 8.2% in 2025 despite the AI boom, limited by export control bottlenecks. The stock has surged 340% from its offering price of $12.25, pricing in substantial future growth that may not materialize if geopolitical tensions escalate.
