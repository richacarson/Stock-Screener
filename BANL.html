Company Profile
Energy ·Oil & Gas Midstream ·NASDAQ ·Malaysia
CBL International Limited is the listing vehicle of the Banle Group, an established marine fuel logistics company in Asia Pacific providing one-stop vessel refueling solutions. The company facilitates bunkering between ship operators and local physical distributors/traders, purchasing and coordinating marine fuel delivery across Malaysia, Hong Kong, China, South Korea, Singapore, and internationally. Founded in 2015 and headquartered in Kuala Lumpur, it operates as a subsidiary of CBL (Asia) Limited.
37 Employees ·www.banle-intl.com

Investment Thesis
CBL International operates a capital-light marine fuel logistics model that generated $592.5M revenue in FY2024 (up 35.9% YoY), demonstrating the scalability of its bunkering facilitation platform across Asia-Pacific [1]. The strategic pivot into sustainable marine fuels — via the Green Marine acquisition, ISCC certifications, and LNG bunkering capabilities — positions CBL ahead of IMO 2030 decarbonization mandates that will reshape the $200B+ global bunkering market [3]. Dr. Chia's governance recognition (Directors of the Year Award) and EcoVadis Silver Medal suggest a management team building credibility in ESG-driven maritime logistics.
The bear case is severe: CBL is a $13.7M market cap company generating negative net income (-$3.0M TTM) on $538.5M revenue, implying razor-thin negative margins that leave no room for error [4]. Daily liquidity of only $854K makes the stock essentially untradeable for institutional investors and creates extreme price volatility risk. The company has only 37 employees managing half-a-billion in revenue, raising operational risk concentration concerns. Revenue declined 13.3% YoY in recent quarters after the FY2024 surge, suggesting the growth trajectory may be volatile rather than sustainable. As a Malaysian-based sub-$1 micro-cap with no analyst coverage, BANL carries significant information asymmetry and governance risk despite Dr. Chia's awards [2][4].
