Company Profile
Energy ·Oil & Gas E&P ·AMEX ·United States
Battalion Oil Corporation is a small-cap independent energy company focused on the acquisition, production, exploration, and development of onshore oil and natural gas assets in the Delaware Basin of West Texas. Formerly known as Halcon Resources Corporation, the company changed its name in January 2020 and operates with approximately 40 employees, selling crude oil, natural gas, and natural gas liquids to independent marketers and pipeline companies.

Investment Thesis
Battalion Oil is a micro-cap E&P company in severe financial distress, with a $49M market cap, persistent net losses ($64.8M in Q1 2026 alone), and revenue declining at a -22.7% five-year CAGR due to falling commodity prices [1]. The stock has collapsed from $29.70 to $2.23 over the past year, reflecting the market's assessment that the current business model is unsustainable at prevailing oil prices.

However, the $108.3M net debt against a $49M market cap, shareholder-rights investigations following operational problems at the AGI processing facility, and a forward EPS of just $0.23 make this a high-risk speculative position unsuitable for an IOWN portfolio [1][3].
