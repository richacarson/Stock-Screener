Company Profile
Energy ·Oil & Gas Exploration and Production ·NYSE American ·United States
Battalion Oil Corporation is an independent energy company focused on acquiring, developing, and producing onshore liquids-rich oil and natural gas in the Delaware Basin, West Texas. The company operates 27,097 net acres in Ward County (Monument Draw position), following an all-stock acquisition of 7,090 additional acres in March 2026. Full-year 2025 production averaged 12,096 Boe/d (53% oil), with trailing 12-month revenue of $183M. The company is operating under a NYSE American compliance plan after failing listing standards, with a deadline of November 30, 2026.
