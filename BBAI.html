Company Profile
Technology ·Information Technology Services ·NYSE ·United States
BigBear.ai Holdings, Inc. provides AI-powered decision intelligence solutions for national security, supply chain management, and digital identity markets. The company offers computer vision, modeling and simulation, generative AI platforms (Ask Sage), biometric identity solutions, and predictive analytics to government and commercial customers. Formed through mergers of multiple analytics firms, it serves defense, homeland security, and critical infrastructure clients.

Investment Thesis
BigBear.ai is a government-focused AI company pivoting from legacy analytics to a GenAI-first strategy through the Ask Sage acquisition, which has already driven gross margin expansion from 21.3% to 34.0% in Q1 2026. The $282M backlog (up 14% QoQ) and $60M+ in national security contract wins provide revenue visibility, while CEO McAleenan's former role as Acting Secretary of Homeland Security offers unmatched credibility with government buyers.
However, the financial profile is severely distressed: $294M net loss on $128M in declining revenue (-19.3% YoY) in FY2025, -58.4% ROE, and -227% profit margin [1][5]. The company has consistently destroyed value through serial acquisitions without achieving profitability, and the stock at $4.19 is down 55% from its 52-week high of $9.39. The FY2026 guidance of $135-165M represents a return to growth but from a lower base, and the company must demonstrate that Ask Sage can transform the financial model — not just the gross margin line — before it warrants more than a speculative position. The Infrastructure score of 1/10 reflects the reality that innovation alone cannot compensate for persistent massive losses.
