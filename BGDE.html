Company Profile

Big Digital Energy, Inc. (formerly Mawson Infrastructure Group Inc., renamed April 2026) is a technology company that designs, develops, and operates digital infrastructure platforms. The company offers artificial intelligence and high-performance computing colocation, digital asset (Bitcoin) mining, and technology infrastructure services. Based in Midland, Pennsylvania, the company operates data center facilities and is pivoting from pure crypto mining toward AI/HPC colocation. The company has 33 employees and faces Nasdaq delisting risk after stockholders' equity fell below required thresholds.
