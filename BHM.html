Company Profile
Real Estate ·REIT - Residential ·NYSE American ·United States
Bluerock Homes Trust, Inc. is an externally managed REIT that owns and operates a portfolio of institutional residential properties including single-family homes, build-to-rent communities, and other residential communities. The company focuses on knowledge-economy and high-quality-of-life regions of the Sunbelt and Western United States, targeting high disposable income renters by choice through value-add renovations and ground-up development.
