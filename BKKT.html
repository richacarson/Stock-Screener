Company Profile
Technology ·Software - Infrastructure ·NYSE ·United States
Bakkt is a crypto infrastructure company transitioning from a diversified digital asset platform to a pure-play crypto and stablecoin infrastructure provider. Originally spun out of Intercontinental Exchange (ICE), the company offers crypto trading execution, stablecoin payment processing, and is developing merchant checkout and AI-driven money movement tools.
48 Employees ·www.bakkt.com

Investment Thesis
Bakkt offers a high-risk turnaround bet on crypto and stablecoin infrastructure at a compelling forward valuation. The company eliminated all debt, raised $100M in capital, and is pivoting to pure-play crypto infrastructure with a forward P/E of 9.2x based on analyst expectations of $0.96 EPS in 2026 [1][3]. CEO Akshay Naheta (former SoftBank SVP) brings institutional investment credibility, and CPO Ankit Khemka (former Revolut GM) brings consumer fintech execution experience [2]. The stablecoin addressable market is projected to grow from 3% to 20% of cross-border payment volume ($64T) by 2032, and Bakkt Checkout and Bakkt Agent could capture a slice of this flow [2]. The $270M market cap is modest for a company with ICE lineage and institutional-grade regulatory infrastructure.
The bear case is severe. Bakkt warned in 2024 that it might not have enough cash to remain in business, revenue collapsed 83.4% YoY through the restructuring, trailing EPS is -$6.55, and the company has just 48 employees to execute an ambitious pure-play crypto infrastructure strategy [3]. The forward EPS consensus of $0.96 requires a dramatic business inflection that has not yet materialized in reported financials. Bakkt competes against Coinbase (market cap $65B+), Circle (private), and Paxos (private) — all better capitalized and more established in crypto infrastructure [2]. The voluntary surrender of its New York BitLicense raises regulatory credibility concerns [3]. Multiple CEO transitions in 3 years signal governance instability. Free cash flow remains negative at -$9.9M, and the $29.9M cash position provides limited runway if the pivot takes longer than expected.
