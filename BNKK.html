Company Profile
Financial Services ·Asset Management ·NASDAQ ·United States
Bonk, Inc. (formerly Safety Shot, Inc.) operates as a digital asset treasury company and consumer products firm. The company holds a 51% revenue-sharing interest in BONK.fun, a decentralized finance platform on the Solana blockchain, and manages beverage brands including Safety Shot (an alcohol-reducing drink) and Yerbaé. Rebranded in October 2025, the company pivoted from consumer health beverages to become a public vehicle for the BONK meme-coin ecosystem.

Investment Thesis
Bonk, Inc. is a speculative micro-cap ($22M market cap) that pivoted from a failing health beverage business to become a publicly traded vehicle for exposure to the BONK meme-coin ecosystem on Solana. The company projects 100% revenue growth in 2026, driven by its 51% revenue-sharing interest in BONK.fun (which generated $3.5M in Q1 platform revenue, 233% of internal targets) and ~$4M expected from the Yerbaé beverage brand [1]. The company enters 2026 debt-free with an mNAV of 1.85x and only 5.3M shares outstanding post-reverse split, creating potential for significant per-share value if crypto markets remain favorable.
The bear case is substantial and multifaceted. The company has 10 employees, no profitability (0% profit margin, negative ROE), and a history of serial rebranding that signals strategic instability [4]. Revenue is almost entirely dependent on BONK meme-coin ecosystem activity, which is inherently speculative and subject to extreme volatility — the stock plummeted 48% after initial BONK treasury announcements as investors questioned the strategy [3]. Governance concerns include board reshuffles, CFO resignation, Nasdaq delisting warnings, and FDA scrutiny over unproven Safety Shot efficacy claims [3]. The 1-for-35 reverse stock split and $201K average daily dollar volume make the stock effectively untradeable for institutional investors. With no earnings, no dividends, and a business model entirely dependent on meme-coin speculation, BNKK fails the IOWN framework on nearly every dimension.
