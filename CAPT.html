Company Profile
Basic Materials ·Building Materials ·OTC ·South Korea
Captivision Inc. develops, manufactures, installs, and sells G-Glass, an architectural media glass that combines IT building material with architectural glass for digital out-of-home media, entertainment, and marketing applications. The company operates in Asia Pacific, Europe, Middle East, Africa, and North America. In April 2026, Captivision announced Nasdaq delisting to OTC and signed a letter of intent to acquire Montana Tunnels Mining, intending to pivot to gold mining operations.
