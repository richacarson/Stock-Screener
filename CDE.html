Company Profile
Basic Materials ·Gold ·NYSE ·United States
Coeur Mining, Inc. is a mid-tier precious metals producer operating gold and silver mines in the Americas. Following the transformative merger with SilverCrest Metals in 2024, Coeur operates five mines including Rochester (Nevada), Kensington (Alaska), Wharf (South Dakota), Palmarejo (Mexico), and Las Chispas (Mexico).

Investment Thesis
Coeur Mining is a mid-tier gold and silver producer riding a historic precious metals bull market, with Q1 2026 revenue surging 138% YoY to $856M and net income exploding 640% to $247M [1]. The transformative SilverCrest merger is driving an 80% increase in gold production to ~750,000 ounces in 2026, while the nearly debt-free balance sheet (D/E 7.4%) provides exceptional financial flexibility [1].
At a forward P/E of just 9.0x with management projecting $3B+ EBITDA and $2B free cash flow for 2026, Coeur appears significantly undervalued if current gold prices hold [1]. However, the investment is fundamentally a leveraged bet on gold and silver prices—Coeur's earnings are highly cyclical and would compress dramatically in a metals downturn. The company recently announced a new shareholder payout plan, signaling confidence in sustained cash generation [1]. The Inspire score of 57 (ALIGNED) reflects responsible mining practices with no negative attributions.
