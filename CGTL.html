Company Profile
Consumer Cyclical ·Specialty Retail ·NASDAQ ·Hong Kong
Creative Global Technology Holdings Limited is an investment holding company that sources and resells recycled consumer electronic devices including smartphones, tablets, and laptops, facilitating the flow of retired devices from affluent markets like the United States and Japan to developing regions seeking affordable technology. Founded in 2016 and headquartered in Kwun Tong, Hong Kong, the company IPO'd on the Nasdaq Capital Market in November 2024.
12 Employees ·www.creativeglobaltechnology.com

Investment Thesis
CGTL operates in the growing global refurbished electronics market, which is projected to reach $150B+ by 2030 driven by sustainability trends and demand for affordable technology in developing markets. The company's gross margin improvement to 17.8% from 9.9% in FY2024 suggests some success in shifting toward higher-margin orders, and the extremely low D/E ratio of 0.1% indicates minimal balance sheet leverage risk [1].
The bear case is severe: TTM revenue has collapsed to $21.2M with a net loss of $13.4M, the company faces Nasdaq delisting risk after failing to maintain a $1.00 bid price, and with only 12 employees and a $30M market cap, CGTL lacks the scale, brand recognition, and operational infrastructure to compete against well-funded platforms like Back Market [2]. The company has no institutional investor backing, no analyst coverage, and extremely thin liquidity that makes it unsuitable for any meaningful portfolio allocation.
