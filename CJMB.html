Company Profile
Industrials ·Integrated Freight & Logistics ·NASDAQ ·United States
Callan JMB is a vertically integrated logistics company providing thermal management and cold chain logistics solutions to the life sciences industry and emergency management agencies. Services include frozen shipping for personalized medicine, cell therapies, vaccines, diagnostic materials, and biopharmaceuticals, as well as emergency preparedness services including medical stockpile management. The company recently pivoted to GLP-1 pharmaceutical cold-chain infrastructure and partnered with Attune Biotech for clinical trial manufacturing oversight.
