Company Profile
Healthcare ·Healthcare Plans ·NASDAQ ·United States
Clover Health Investments, Corp. provides Medicare Advantage plans in the United States, offering PPO and HMO plans to Medicare-eligible individuals. The company operates Clover Assistant, an AI-powered software platform that helps physicians detect, identify, and manage chronic diseases. Founded in 2014 and headquartered in Wilmington, Delaware.

Investment Thesis
Clover Health is an AI-powered Medicare Advantage insurer approaching a critical inflection point: management guides for first-ever full-year GAAP profitability in 2026 on $2.8-2.9B revenue, driven by 53% AEP membership growth and maturing cohort economics [1]. The Clover Assistant platform provides genuine technological differentiation in physician-level clinical decision support, and the Counterpart Health SaaS extension opens a second revenue stream beyond insurance [2].
However, the investment case requires significant faith in execution. The company has less than one year of cash runway, profit margins remain negative, and ROE is deeply negative at -26% [1]. Competition from UnitedHealth and Humana's own AI investments threatens Clover's differentiation, while CMS regulatory changes to risk adjustment models could compress reimbursements [3]. At a $900M market cap and $1.72 share price, CLOV is a speculative turnaround story where the AI-driven care model must prove scalable profitability to justify current valuation.
