Company Profile
Financial Services ·Capital Markets ·NASDAQ ·United States
CleanSpark, Inc. operates as a bitcoin mining company in the Americas. The company owns, leases, and operates data centers and power assets supporting Bitcoin mining. Formerly known as Stratean Inc., CleanSpark is pivoting toward AI data center infrastructure while maintaining its core Bitcoin mining operations with 585 MW of ERCOT-approved capacity.

Investment Thesis
CleanSpark is a speculative Bitcoin mining company attempting a high-stakes pivot toward AI/HPC data center infrastructure. The company holds significant power assets (585 MW ERCOT-approved) and $925M in Bitcoin holdings as of March 2026, but operates at deep losses with -67.7% profit margins, -34.8% ROE, and a $378M quarterly net loss [1]. The AI data center pivot is strategically logical — leveraging existing power infrastructure for higher-margin workloads — but execution risk is substantial given unproven management (CEO <1 year tenure), poor employee culture (42% Glassdoor recommendation), and zero AI revenue to date [5][7].
At $13.47/share with a $3.5B market cap, CLSK is priced on hope that the AI pivot will succeed and Bitcoin holdings will appreciate. The clean energy profile (90%+ renewable) and Inspire score of 63 are positives [4], but the fundamental financial picture — negative earnings, -24.9% revenue decline, $1.79B debt against $260M cash — warrants extreme caution. With an overall IOWN score of 27, CleanSpark earns a SELL recommendation. This is a speculative asset dependent on Bitcoin price recovery and successful AI infrastructure buildout, not a quality investment by IOWN standards.
