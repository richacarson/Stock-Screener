Company Profile
Utilities ·Utilities - Renewable ·NASDAQ ·United States
Smart Powerr Corp. is a Nevada-incorporated holding company that conducts operations through PRC subsidiaries in the waste energy recycling industry in China. Formerly known as China Recycling Energy Corporation (renamed March 2022), the company provides energy saving and recovery facilities for energy-intensive industries, including waste gas power generation, waste heat power generation, and biomass power generation using Build-Operate-Transfer structures.
