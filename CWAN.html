Investment Thesis
Clearwater Analytics is building the definitive institutional investment management platform with 77% revenue growth, 98% gross retention, and 110% net retention across $10T+ in assets. The CWAN GenAI deployment is driving gross margins to 82% on core clients, reaching 2027 targets two years early. The pending $8.4B take-private by Permira/Warburg Pincus at a premium validates the platform value, while 2026 revenue estimates of $950M suggest continued strong growth from acquisition integration and organic expansion.
The bear case centers on execution risk from integrating three major acquisitions simultaneously (Enfusion, Beacon, Bistro) while the company remains GAAP unprofitable with $860M in debt. The D+ executive team rating and cultural concerns raise integration risk. The $44M trade secrets verdict to SS&C creates legal precedent concerns. If the take-private deal closes, upside is capped; if it fails, the stock faces significant downside given current premium pricing. Forward P/E of 26x on expected profitability leaves little margin for error.
