Company Profile

Delixy Holdings Limited is a Singapore-based investment holding company engaged in the wholesale trading of crude oil and oil-based products across Southeast Asia, East Asia, and the Middle East. Incorporated in 2024 and listed on NASDAQ in July 2025, the company is a subsidiary of Mega Origin Holdings Limited and is expanding into LNG trading.
