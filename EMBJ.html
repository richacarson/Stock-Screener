Investment Thesis
Embraer is a best-in-class aerospace manufacturer experiencing a structural growth inflection with record backlogs ($32.1B), accelerating deliveries, and expanding margins. The company's dominant position in regional aviation, diversified defense/executive segments, and innovation pipeline create a compelling multi-year growth story trading at a reasonable forward multiple.
The 2026 revenue guidance of $8.2-8.5B represents continued double-digit growth supported by 240-255 aircraft deliveries. Margin expansion from operational leverage and services mix shift should drive earnings growth well above revenue growth. Key watch items include supply chain execution risk, Brazilian currency exposure, and the pace of Eve Air Mobility commercialization. The stock deserves a premium multiple given visibility and execution track record.
Key Catalysts
  1. Record $32.1B backlog (sixth consecutive all-time high, +22% YoY) provides multi-year revenue visibility and delivery acceleration
  2. Margin expansion from services & support revenue mix shift and manufacturing scale economies toward 9%+ EBIT margin target
  3. Defense & Security segment growing 36% YoY with expanding international military contracts beyond Brazil
Key Risks
  1. Supply chain constraints in aerospace (engines, avionics) could delay deliveries and compress near-term margins
  2. Brazilian Real currency volatility creates earnings translation risk for USD-reporting investors
  3. High trailing P/E of 32.6x leaves limited margin of safety if growth execution disappoints or tariffs escalate beyond 10%
