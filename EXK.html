Company Profile
Basic Materials ·Silver ·NYSE ·Canada
Endeavour Silver is a growing mid-tier silver mining company operating the Terronera mine in Jalisco, Mexico (commercial production commenced October 2025), the Guanacevi mine in Durango, Mexico, and the Kolpa mine in Peru (acquired May 2025). The company divested Bolanitos in January 2026 and is developing the large-scale Pitarrilla silver project. Q1 2026 revenue surged 230% YoY to $209.7M driven by Terronera ramp-up and elevated silver prices.

Investment Thesis
Endeavour Silver is at an inflection point: Q1 2026 delivered $209.7M revenue (+230% YoY) and $64.9M net income ($0.21 EPS, beating estimates by 40%) as Terronera and Kolpa ramped production to 3.34M silver equivalent ounces (+78% YoY) [1]. Forward P/E of 8.2x is deeply discounted versus peers, and 2026 guidance of 14.6-15.6M AgEq oz at AISC of $27-28/oz implies substantial margin expansion from the $41.19/oz achieved in late 2025. D/E of 39% is conservative, $215M cash provides balance sheet flexibility, and the Pitarrilla development project offers decades-long production growth potential. Silver's structural demand growth from AI data centers, solar panels, and electronics creates a favorable macro backdrop, with a 67M oz projected 2026 market deficit [2].
The bear case centers on commodity price exposure, operational execution risk, and trailing financial weakness. Trailing ROE of -3.9% and profit margin of -3.5% reflect the loss-making FY2025 period dominated by $126.2M in derivative contract losses [1]. The Terronera mine experienced security stoppages in February 2026 and ongoing electrical disruption risk in Mexico adds operational uncertainty. Mexico's evolving mining law — stricter regulations, reduced concession duration, water use prioritization — creates permitting and resource nationalism risk [3]. The $350M convertible debt adds leverage, and silver price sensitivity is acute: if silver falls below $28-30/oz, AISC breakeven becomes marginal. The company does not pay a dividend, and the Inspire Insight score of 0 with no positive or negative attributions provides no faith-based differentiation.
