Company Profile
Healthcare ·Biotechnology ·NASDAQ ·United States
FibroBiologics is a clinical-stage biotechnology company developing fibroblast cell-based therapies for chronic diseases. The pipeline includes CYWC628 (wound healing, Phase 1/2 in Australia), CYMS101 (multiple sclerosis, Phase 1 completed), CYPS317 (psoriasis, IND pending), and preclinical programs in disc degeneration, longevity, and cancer. The company holds 270+ US and international patents/patents pending.
