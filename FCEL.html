Company Profile
Industrials ·Electrical Equipment & Parts ·NASDAQ ·United States
FuelCell Energy designs, manufactures, sells, installs, operates, and services stationary fuel cell power plants for distributed baseload power generation using its proprietary molten carbonate fuel cell (MCFC) technology. The company serves commercial, industrial, government, and utility customers and is expanding into AI data center power with its 12.5MW Power Block platform. Founded in 1969, the company is headquartered in Danbury, Connecticut.
