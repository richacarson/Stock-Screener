Investment Thesis
Faraday Future is a speculative micro-cap ($109M market cap) in existential crisis that has pivoted from failed luxury EVs to Embodied AI robotics. Q1 2026 showed early signs of life with $512K revenue (62% YoY growth) and 68 robots shipped with positive unit gross margins, but the going-concern doubt flagged in SEC filings and only $12.2M in cash create immediate survival risk.
The raised 2026 robot shipment target of 1,500 units would represent meaningful progress if achieved, but Faraday's 12-year history of missed timelines, governance crises, and serial dilution provides no basis for confidence. The company trades at $0.36/share with persistent NASDAQ delisting risk and competes against Tesla Optimus, Boston Dynamics, and Figure AI with a fraction of their resources. This is a lottery ticket with heavily unfavorable odds rather than a legitimate investment.
