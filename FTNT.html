The 2026 guidance of $7.5-7.7B revenue (12% growth) and $8.4-8.6B billings (13% growth) with 33-36% operating margins reflects disciplined, profitable growth. The bear case acknowledges the 24.6x forward P/E is premium pricing for 12% revenue growth guidance, and the forward PEG of 1.89 suggests fair-to-full valuation. ROE of 135.7% is artificially inflated by negative stockholders' equity from aggressive buybacks, masking true capital efficiency. Supply chain pressures requiring 6 months of inventory buildup and 5-20% price increases could impact demand if customers delay purchases. SecOps billings deceleration in Q4 (management attributed to deal timing) bears monitoring, and competition from Palo Alto Networks' platformization strategy could compress Fortinet's market share in SASE and SecOps.
