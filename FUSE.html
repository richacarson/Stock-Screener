Company Profile
Technology ·Software - Application ·NASDAQ ·United States
Fusemachines Inc. develops and delivers artificial intelligence as a service and machine learning software solutions. Products include Fuse Anna (AI assistant), Fuse Prospector (AI sales platform), AI Studio, and AI Engines. The company also operates the Fusemachines AI Fellowship program for underserved communities. Founded in 2013, the company listed on NASDAQ in October 2025 through a SPAC merger with CSLM Acquisition Corp.
