Company Profile
Technology ·Software - Application ·NYSE ·United Kingdom
Genius Sports Limited is a sports data and technology company that provides data management, video streaming, and integrity services to sports leagues, betting operators, and media companies worldwide. The company's GeniusIQ AI platform captures and processes next-generation sports data using machine learning and generative AI. Key partnerships include the NFL, Premier League, NCAA, and major global sports organizations. Founded in 2016 and headquartered in London, with operations across 20+ countries.

Investment Thesis
Genius Sports delivered Q1 2026 revenue of $188M (31% YoY growth) with adjusted EBITDA of $24.1M, then raised full-year 2026 guidance to $990M-$1.01B revenue and $270-$280M adjusted EBITDA (28% margin) following the Legend acquisition close [1]. The company's exclusive data partnerships with the NFL, Premier League, and NCAA create structural moats that competitors cannot replicate, while the GeniusIQ AI platform — now led by former Google CTO Mark Kropf — positions Genius Sports at the intersection of sports data and generative AI [2]. With a nearly debt-free balance sheet ($197M cash, $30.6M debt), positive operating cash flow of $50.8M, and insider purchases of 1.4M shares at depressed prices, the fundamental trajectory is improving rapidly toward sustainable profitability.
The stock has declined 46% over the past 52 weeks despite strong operational performance, reflecting market skepticism about the path to GAAP profitability and the Legend integration execution risk [4]. At $1.46B market cap on a $1B revenue run-rate, the valuation appears undemanding, but persistent GAAP losses (-$158.8M TTM net income) and 11% YoY share dilution erode shareholder value. The gambling exposure carries regulatory risk — changes to sports betting legalization, advertising restrictions, or responsible gambling requirements could constrain the total addressable market. The Legend acquisition increases integration complexity and raises the execution bar. Forward P/E of 29.31 assumes a profitability inflection that has not yet occurred, and the 1.92 beta means any market downturn will amplify losses in this name.
