Investment Thesis
Bull case: GITS is a tiny lottery ticket on global K-pop fandom monetization via FANTOO, Faning, and Koreavibe.net, with recent music IP deals (ATEEZ theme song for 'The Legend of MegaRace', Kang Daniel partnership) providing cultural visibility [1][2]. The global K-culture fandom TAM is growing, and a successful platform pivot could produce outsized returns from a ~$10M market cap. The company has regained Nasdaq listing compliance after multiple delinquency notices.
Bear case dominates. Q3 2025 revenue was just $1,838 with TTM EPS of -$2.34 and operating cash flow of -$1.68M — the company is effectively pre-revenue on a commercial basis [3]. Nasdaq filing delinquencies in multiple consecutive years signal governance weakness and forced ticker changes (HRYU to GITS December 2024) reflect stigma risk [2][3].
