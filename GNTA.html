Company Profile
Healthcare ·Biotechnology ·NASDAQ ·Italy
Genenta Science is a clinical-stage biotechnology company developing hematopoietic stem cell gene therapies for solid tumors, headquartered in Milan, Italy. Its lead product Temferon is in Phase 1/2a clinical trials for glioblastoma multiforme. In January 2026, the company announced a strategic transformation into a defense, aerospace, and national security industrial consolidator under Italy's Golden Power legislation, planning to adopt the corporate name Saentra Forge. Incorporated in 2014.
