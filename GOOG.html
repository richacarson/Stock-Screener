Investment Thesis
Alphabet is compounding at an accelerating rate: Q1 2026 revenue grew 22% to $109.9B (11th consecutive quarter of double-digit growth), net income surged 81% to $62.6B ($5.11 EPS), and Google Cloud crossed $20B quarterly revenue growing 63% YoY [2]. The company operates with a fortress balance sheet — $126.8B cash, 38.9% ROE, 37.9% profit margins — and generated $160B+ annual operating cash flow in FY2025 [1]. At forward P/E of 26.6x with 34.5% EPS growth, Alphabet offers rare growth-at-reasonable-price characteristics for a $4.7T market cap company. The $175-185B 2026 capex is building durable AI infrastructure moats that should compound returns for a decade [2].
The primary bear risk is structural: the DOJ antitrust suit targeting Google's search and advertising monopoly could force divestiture of Chrome, Android, or default search agreements with Apple ($15-20B annually) [5]. AI-driven search disruption from ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude is beginning to cannibalize high-intent queries — if AI assistants bypass traditional search at scale, Alphabet's highest-margin ad engine faces structural decline [5]. Key Risks

  3. AI search disruption: ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude are reducing click-through rates on traditional search for certain query types — structural shift from search to conversational AI could erode Google's highest-margin ad inventory over time [5]
