Company Profile

Gaxos.ai Inc. is a micro-cap company developing AI-powered applications across multiple segments: Gaxos Labs (UnGPT.ai text humanizer, Art-Gen.AI image/video creation, Bible Pray AI spiritual app, AI Sales Coaching Platform), Gaxos Health (RNK Health subsidiary providing medications and wellness products), and a 19.99% stake in America First Defense.AI (counter-drone systems). Formerly The NFT Gaming Company, the firm rebranded in January 2024. Headquartered in Roseland, New Jersey with 3 full-time employees and Q1 2026 revenue of $1.81M against a net loss of $2.47M.

Cash of $1.51M plus $10.2M in corporate bonds provides roughly 12 months of runway at the current burn rate, but the bonds carry unrealized losses of $358K. The strategy is scattered across AI tools, health, defense, gaming, and spiritual apps with no focus. All AI products are thin wrappers over commoditizing third-party AI models with no proprietary technology moat [1][2].
