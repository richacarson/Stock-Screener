Investment Thesis
Harmony Biosciences trades at an exceptionally low forward P/E of 4.8x with WAKIX on track for blockbuster $1B+ revenue in 2026, $882.5M cash with minimal debt, and five Phase 3 trials across five indications providing significant pipeline optionality. Revenue growth of 21% in 2025 with 20% ROE demonstrates strong commercial execution. Generic protection through 2030 (potentially 2044 via pitolisant GR) provides revenue visibility, while the pediatric cataplexy approval expands the addressable market. The Inspire score of 42 supports faith alignment.
The bear case centers on single-product concentration — WAKIX represented ~100% of 2025 revenue, creating binary risk if competition emerges or pricing pressure intensifies. GAAP EPS declined 55% in 2025 due to pipeline investment, and five Phase 3 trials create significant clinical failure risk. The FDA rejected the idiopathic hypersomnia label expansion, limiting near-term addressable market growth. At 293 employees, Harmony lacks the infrastructure to fully commercialize multiple products simultaneously. The orexin-2 receptor agonist class represents both an opportunity (BP1.15205) and a threat (competitors' programs) to the narcolepsy franchise.
