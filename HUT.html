Investment Thesis
Hut 8 offers a unique pure-play on AI infrastructure with an 8,500 MW development pipeline, the $7.0B Google-backed River Bend data center project, and 13,696 BTC ($1.6B) providing Bitcoin upside optionality. Revenue grew 45% in 2025 with Q4 gross margin improving to 60%, and analyst models suggest HPC revenue could surpass $250M annually by late 2026. The stock gained 298% over the past year, reflecting market enthusiasm for the AI infrastructure pivot. The American Bitcoin carve-out simplifies the investment thesis toward contracted infrastructure cash flows.
The bear case is severe: the company posted a net loss of $301.8M in Q4 2025, has negative free cash flow, negative ROE of -18.6%, and a balance sheet where $1.6B in volatile BTC holdings dwarf $45M in cash. With a beta of 6.17, the stock is extremely volatile. The AI data center pivot is largely aspirational — only 330 MW of 8,500 MW is under construction, and revenue remains dominated by Bitcoin mining. Execution risk on multi-billion-dollar construction projects is substantial for a 248-person company. If Bitcoin prices decline or AI spending slows, both revenue streams face simultaneous pressure.
