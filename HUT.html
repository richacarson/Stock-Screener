Company Profile
Technology ·Bitcoin Mining / AI Data Center Infrastructure ·NASDAQ ·United States
Hut 8 Corp. is an energy infrastructure platform integrating power, digital infrastructure, and compute at scale. Originally a Bitcoin mining company, Hut 8 has pivoted aggressively toward AI data center infrastructure, securing $16.8B in contracted revenue across two hyperscale AI campuses (Beacon Point 1 GW and River Bend) through triple-net, take-or-pay leases with high-investment-grade tenants including partnerships with Anthropic and Google-backed Fluidstack. Also operates American Bitcoin, a majority-owned subsidiary for industrial-scale Bitcoin mining. Founded in 2023, headquartered in Miami, Florida.

Investment Thesis
Hut 8 has executed a remarkable transformation from Bitcoin mining to AI data center infrastructure, securing $16.8B in contracted revenue through triple-net, take-or-pay leases across two hyperscale campuses [2]. The Beacon Point 352 MW lease ($9.8B, 15-year term) and River Bend 245 MW lease ($7.0B with Fluidstack/Google backing) provide exceptional long-term revenue visibility with high-investment-grade counterparties [2][3]. Q1 2026 revenue grew 226% YoY to $71M, and the $3.25B investment-grade secured notes financing enables non-dilutive construction. Bitcoin mining operations (817 BTC mined in Q1) and a Bitcoin treasury strategy provide additional optionality. At $12.9B market cap, the contracted $16.8B revenue pipeline implies meaningful embedded value.
The bear case centers on execution risk and current unprofitability. Despite 226% revenue growth, Hut 8 posted a $253M net loss in Q1 2026, driven by $295.7M in digital asset losses, heavy depreciation, and negative operating margins in the digital infrastructure segment [1]. Material AI data center revenue is not expected until Q2 2027, meaning the company must execute massive construction projects on time and on budget. The negative PEG ratio of -0.40 reflects declining near-term earnings expectations, and ROE of -18.6% indicates the company is currently destroying equity value. Bitcoin price exposure creates additional volatility — average revenue per Bitcoin mined fell from $91,512 to $76,077 YoY [1]. The Trump family connection through American Bitcoin adds political and reputational risk, and the $3.25B debt load for construction financing creates leverage risk if AI data center demand softens.
Key Catalysts
  1. Material AI data center revenue expected Q2 2027 from Beacon Point and River Bend campuses, with $16.8B in contracted revenue providing 15-year visibility from triple-net leases
  2. Anthropic partnership for 245-2,295 MW of AI infrastructure capacity represents expansion optionality that could multiply the contracted revenue base as AI compute demand accelerates
  3. Bitcoin treasury strategy and American Bitcoin subsidiary provide optionality to any sustained crypto bull market, with 817 BTC mined in Q1 2026 at improving mining efficiency
Key Risks
  1. Q1 2026 net loss of $253M on $71M revenue with ROE of -18.6% — the company is currently unprofitable with material AI revenue not expected until Q2 2027, creating a 12+ month execution gap
  2. $3.25B in secured debt for data center construction creates leverage risk if projects face delays, cost overruns, or if AI infrastructure demand moderates from current elevated expectations
  3. Bitcoin price exposure remains significant — average revenue per BTC mined fell 17% YoY, and $295.7M in digital asset losses in Q1 demonstrate the volatility of the crypto treasury strategy
