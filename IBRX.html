Investment Thesis
ImmunityBio offers exposure to a potentially transformative immunotherapy platform with ANKTIVA revenue surging 168% YoY to a record $44M in Q1 2026, building on 700% full-year 2025 growth to $113M [1]. Analyst consensus projects $230M revenue for full-year 2026, and the company has expanded to 33 countries with Saudi NSCLC approval and NCCN guideline inclusion [1][3]. Cash of $381M provides meaningful runway, the AI-powered Leonardo NK-cell manufacturing robot nears completion, and multiple label expansion catalysts create a dense 2026 catalyst calendar [2].
The bear case is significant: GAAP net loss ballooned to $632.8M in Q1 2026 (vs. $129.6M Q1 2025), FDA warning letters and lawsuits test the Anktiva growth story, and Glassdoor reviews (2.7/5) reveal a 'demoralizing' internal culture with frequent layoffs [1][2]. Single-product concentration risk persists — 100% of revenue comes from ANKTIVA, and any safety signal or competitive displacement would be devastating. Forward PE of 77x prices in substantial success, and Dr. Soon-Shiong's controversial corporate history (NantKwest, NantHealth) raises governance concerns. The stock's significant appreciation already reflects optimistic consensus scenarios.
