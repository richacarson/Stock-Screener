Company Profile
Industrials ·Marine Shipping ·NASDAQ ·Greece
Icon Energy Corp. is an international shipping company providing worldwide seaborne transportation services for dry bulk cargoes via a fleet of oceangoing vessels. The company operates three vessels (including M/V Charlie added June 2025) on time charters linked to Baltic shipping indices. Incorporated in 2023 and based in Athens, Greece.
