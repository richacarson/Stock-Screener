Company Profile
Healthcare ·Biotechnology ·OTC ·Denmark
IO Biotech, Inc. was a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company developing immune-modulating therapeutic cancer vaccines based on its proprietary T-win technology platform. The lead candidate IO102-IO103 (Cylembio) targeted cancer cells and immune-suppressive cells expressing IDO and PD-L1. The company filed Chapter 7 bankruptcy on March 31, 2026, after its Phase 3 trial missed statistical significance and the FDA recommended against BLA submission. All employees have been terminated, all directors have resigned, and the stock was delisted from Nasdaq effective June 18, 2026. Shares trade on OTC Pink Sheets as IOBTQ at approximately $0.006.
