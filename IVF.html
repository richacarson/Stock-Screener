INVO Fertility, Inc. is a healthcare fertility company focused on the acquisition and operation of fertility clinics and the commercialization of INVOcell, a proprietary FDA-cleared medical device that enables fertilization and early embryo development within the woman's body (in vivo). The company reported Q1 2026 revenue of $2.02M (up 23% YoY) and operates a growing clinic network. Market cap is approximately $2.36M with a going-concern warning and $96.9M accumulated deficit.
