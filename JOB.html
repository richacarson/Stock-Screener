Company Profile

GEE Group Inc. provides human resources staffing solutions across the United States through over 30 offices in 15 states. The company operates through Industrial Staffing and Professional Staffing segments, placing IT, accounting, finance, engineering, and medical scribe professionals for direct hire and contract assignments under brands including Access Data Consulting, Agile Resources, Paladin Consulting, and Staffing Now.
