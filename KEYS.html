Q1 FY2026 delivered a blowout: $1.6B revenue (up 23%), $1.65B orders (up 30%), and record $2.8B backlog. Tariff exposure across global operations creates margin risk, and the Inspire MISALIGNED score (-49) from LGBT Activism may concern faith-based investors. If AI infrastructure capex normalizes, the current order acceleration could reverse as quickly as it materialized.
