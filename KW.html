Company Profile
Real Estate ·Real Estate Services ·NYSE ·United States
Kennedy-Wilson Holdings, Inc. is a global real estate investment company with $36 billion in assets under management, focusing on multifamily rental housing, industrial properties, and real estate debt origination. Founded in 1977 and headquartered in Beverly Hills, California, the company operates across the U.S., U.K., Ireland, Spain, Jersey, and Japan. In February 2026, KW entered into a definitive agreement to be acquired by a consortium led by Chairman/CEO William McMorrow and Fairfax Financial Holdings at $10.90 per share.
