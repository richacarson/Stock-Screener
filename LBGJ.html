Company Profile
Industrials ·Specialty Industrial Machinery ·NASDAQ ·China
Li Bang International Corporation Inc. designs, develops, produces, and sells stainless-steel commercial kitchen equipment under the Li Bang brand in China. Products include cooking machinery, food machinery, hotel supplies, kitchen accessories, steaming cabinets, fume purifiers, and waste processors. The company also provides kitchen design, construction, installation, and after-sales services to hotels, companies, schools, hospitals, and government facilities. Founded in 1992 and based in Jiangyin, China.
