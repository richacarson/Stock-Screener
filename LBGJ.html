Company Profile
Industrials ·Specialty Industrial Machinery ·NASDAQ ·China
Li Bang International Corporation Inc. designs, develops, produces, and sells stainless-steel commercial kitchen equipment under the Li Bang brand in China. Founded in 1992 and incorporated in the Cayman Islands in 2021, the company offers steaming, cooking, baking, frying, disinfection, and refrigeration equipment to hotels, schools, hospitals, institutions, and enterprises. Based in Jiangyin, China, Li Bang is majority-controlled by CEO and Chairman Feng Huang through Maple Huang Holdings Limited, which owns approximately 75.3% of voting power. The company IPO'd on Nasdaq in 2022, priced below range at $4, and has traded below $1 for most of 2025-2026, requiring a 1-for-100 reverse stock split to regain Nasdaq minimum bid compliance.
