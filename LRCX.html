Investment Thesis
Lam Research is the premier picks-and-shovels play on the AI semiconductor revolution, with record FY2025 revenue of $20.6B (+27% YoY) and Q2 FY2026 record revenue of $5.34B driven by insatiable demand for AI chip fabrication equipment. Advanced packaging revenue is growing 40%+ as HBM and chiplet architectures proliferate. The GAA transistor transition adds $1B in SAM per 100K wafer starts, expanding Lam's addressable market to the high-30% range of WFE. Gross margin of 49.7% and profit margin of 30.2% demonstrate exceptional pricing power from technology leadership. The installed base of 100K+ chambers generates record CSBG revenue ($7.2B in CY2025), providing recurring income that grows with every chip generation. The 20% payout ratio provides massive dividend growth runway with 15% 5-year CAGR.
The bear case is cyclicality and valuation. At 47x trailing P/E and 33x forward P/E, LRCX is priced for continued AI-driven growth perfection — any WFE spending deceleration could trigger severe multiple compression. The semiconductor equipment industry has historically experienced 30-50% revenue declines in downturns, and while AI may dampen cyclicality, it cannot eliminate it. WFE guidance of $135B for 2026 was 'tempered by cleanroom constraints,' suggesting supply limitations may constrain near-term growth. The 0.5% dividend yield is negligible for income investors despite the dividend sleeve classification. US-China export controls on advanced semiconductor equipment create regulatory risk for ~30% of revenue. The -49 Inspire score from LGBT activism creates headwinds for faith-aligned investors.
