Investment Thesis
Medicus Pharma is a speculative pre-revenue biotech with a Phase 2 skin cancer asset (SKNJCT-003) and an AI-driven clinical development strategy. The completion of the Phase 2 BCC study and Gorlin Syndrome Alliance collaboration provide modest clinical validation, while the Antev acquisition expands optionality into men's health [2]. CEO Bokhari's $14M personal investment and the company's AI partnership with Reliant AI signal leadership conviction [2]. The $19.3M market cap provides enormous upside leverage if any asset reaches commercialization.
