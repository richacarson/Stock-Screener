Investment Thesis
MakeMyTrip is India's dominant online travel platform with a powerful multi-brand strategy (MakeMyTrip, Goibibo, redBus) capturing $9.1B+ in annual gross bookings across the world's fastest-growing major travel market [1]. Q3 FY2026 revenue of $295.7M grew 10.6% YoY with adjusted operating profit surpassing $50M for the first time, demonstrating improving unit economics [1]. The company's 5-year revenue CAGR of 48% reflects India's secular digitization trend — online travel penetration remains below 40%, providing a long runway for growth. Free cash flow of $127M and $814M in cash (vs. $1.4B debt) provide financial flexibility for continued expansion into international markets and ancillary services [5].
The bear case centers on valuation, profitability, and disruption risk. At 92x trailing P/E and a PEG of 5.39, MakeMyTrip is priced for growth acceleration that the 10.6% YoY revenue growth does not yet justify — the stock has declined 58% from its 52-week high of $113.85 [5]. Profit margin of 5.5% is razor-thin for a platform business, and EPS declined 69.6% YoY, indicating the path to sustainable profitability is not linear. Generative AI poses a genuine structural threat to OTA intermediaries as AI trip planning tools improve — if consumers bypass MakeMyTrip to book directly through AI assistants, the entire OTA model faces compression [2]. Competition from Booking.com's India push, Flipkart's Cleartrip, and Google's travel products intensifies pricing pressure. Currency risk (INR/USD) adds volatility for US-listed investors [5].

AI Risk Protection (4/9): MakeMyTrip faces meaningful AI disruption risk. Generative AI platforms (Google Gemini, ChatGPT, Perplexity) are increasingly capable of trip planning, price comparison, and booking — functions that constitute MakeMyTrip's core value proposition [2]. If consumers begin using AI assistants to plan and book travel directly, OTA intermediaries could face disintermediation. The company's defense lies in its proprietary hotel/bus supply network, payment integrations, and post-booking service infrastructure that AI tools cannot yet replicate. Indian market specifics (regional languages, fragmented supply) provide additional protection. AI Opportunity (1/1): MakeMyTrip actively deploys AI through Myra.AI chatbot for customer service and ML-powered personalization, generating operational efficiency and improved conversion rates [2].
