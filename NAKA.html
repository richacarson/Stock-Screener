Investment Thesis
Nakamoto Inc. is a Bitcoin treasury and media holding company created through a reverse merger of the former Kindly MD healthcare clinic with Nakamoto Holdings, completed in late 2025. The February 2026 acquisition of BTC Inc (Bitcoin Magazine, Bitcoin Conference) and UTXO Management adds ~$80.5M in consolidated revenue and $34.2M EBITDA from the acquired businesses [1]. The company holds a Bitcoin treasury worth over $500M (~5,400 BTC) [2].
The fundamental problem is governance and value destruction: shares are down 90% YTD, CEO David Bailey orchestrated a related-party acquisition of his own companies at shareholder expense, and the standalone Kindly MD business generated only $1.98M in revenue against $209M in debt [1][2]. The company has 23 employees and no proprietary technology—it is essentially a leveraged Bitcoin bet wrapped in a public shell. The $155M market cap is dwarfed by MicroStrategy and other Bitcoin treasury vehicles with superior scale and governance. Analyst 'Strong Buy' ratings from 2 analysts appear disconnected from the 90% share price decline.
Key Catalysts
  1. Consolidation of BTC Inc and UTXO Management revenue (~$80.5M) into financial statements could revalue the stock [1]
  2. Bitcoin price appreciation would directly increase treasury value and market cap [2]
  3. Bitcoin Conference and Bitcoin Magazine provide recurring revenue and brand recognition in the Bitcoin ecosystem [2]
Key Risks
  1. CEO David Bailey's related-party acquisitions create severe governance risk and potential for continued insider self-dealing [2]
  2. 90% YTD stock decline signals market has lost confidence; massive dilution from 364.8M shares issued to BTC Inc holders [1]
  3. Bitcoin price decline would devastate the treasury-dependent business model with $209M in debt on the balance sheet [1]
