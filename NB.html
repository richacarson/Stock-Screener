Company Profile
Basic Materials ·Other Industrial Metals & Mining ·NASDAQ ·United States
NioCorp Developments Ltd. is a pre-revenue critical minerals development company advancing the Elk Creek Project in southeast Nebraska, which aims to produce niobium, scandium, titanium, and rare earth elements. The company holds 75% of projected niobium output under offtake agreements and has contracted 12 tons/year of scandium production. NioCorp acquired aluminum-scandium alloy manufacturing assets in December 2025 and is pursuing up to $780M in EXIM Bank financing to fund mine construction. With $419M in cash and only 7 employees, NioCorp remains firmly in the exploration and development stage.
