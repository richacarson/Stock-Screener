Nebius Group N.V. is a full-stack AI infrastructure company that builds large-scale GPU clusters, cloud platforms, and developer tools to service the global AI industry. Formerly Yandex N.V., the company rebranded in August 2024 and also operates TripleTen (edtech for tech career reskilling) and Avride (autonomous driving and delivery robotics), with a strategic alliance with NVIDIA to expand AI cloud infrastructure.

Investment Thesis
Nebius is a pure-play AI infrastructure company experiencing hypergrowth — 684% YoY revenue growth to $399M in Q1 2026 with full-year guidance of $3.0-3.4B and ARR targets of $7-9B [1]. The 0.63 PEG ratio and NVIDIA strategic alliance position NBIS as a potentially undervalued AI infrastructure play despite the elevated 608x forward P/E. The core AI cloud business achieved 45% adjusted EBITDA margins, demonstrating unit economics can scale with volume. The 94% low-carbon energy usage and 20% TCO advantage create sustainable competitive differentiation against hyperscaler incumbents [3].
The primary risk is valuation and execution at unprecedented scale. The $20-25B capex commitment dwarfs current revenue, creating substantial financial risk if AI infrastructure demand plateaus or shifts to hyperscaler-dominated models [1]. The 608x forward P/E assumes perfection in growth execution, and the 131% D/E ratio limits balance sheet flexibility. The company's former identity as Yandex introduces governance and geopolitical perception risks, even post-restructuring. Adjusted net losses of $100.3M in Q1 2026 remind investors that GAAP profitability from operations remains distant despite the $621M GAAP net income inflated by a $780.6M equity investment revaluation gain.
