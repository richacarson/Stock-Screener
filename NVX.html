Company Profile
Industrials ·Electrical Equipment & Parts ·NASDAQ ·Australia
NOVONIX is a battery technology and materials company developing and manufacturing synthetic graphite anode materials for lithium-ion batteries, along with battery cell testing equipment. The company operates from facilities in Chattanooga, Tennessee, targeting EV manufacturers and energy storage systems with its patented all-dry, zero-waste production process.

The company's patented all-dry process delivers 60% lower global warming potential versus Chinese synthetic graphite, aligning with EV OEM sustainability requirements and IRA domestic content provisions [3].
