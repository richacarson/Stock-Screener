Company Profile
Energy ·Uranium ·NYSE ·Canada
NexGen Energy Ltd. is an exploration and development stage company focused on the acquisition, exploration, evaluation, and development of uranium properties in Canada. The company holds a 100% interest in the Rook I project, consisting of 32 contiguous mineral claims totaling approximately 35,065 hectares in the southwestern Athabasca Basin of Saskatchewan, targeting production of 30 million pounds of uranium per year upon completion of a four-year construction period.
142 Employees ·www.nexgenenergy.ca
