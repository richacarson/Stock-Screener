Investment Thesis
Our Bond is an early-stage AI-powered personal security company with a compelling product thesis: democratizing protective services through AI/ML and 24/7 Command Centers. Annual revenue of approximately $10M and a pipeline including a potential $10M+ enterprise deployment with a near-1M employee US employer, a top-3 telecom customer with $2M+ ARR potential, and four international city partnerships worth $2-6M ARR collectively represent meaningful growth optionality [2][3]. The company operates across 28 countries with 1.4M+ security requests processed, demonstrating product-market fit in the enterprise and government segments. At a $10M market cap, the stock is priced for failure — any successful conversion of the enterprise pipeline could drive substantial upside.
The bear case is existential. Bond has negative stockholders' equity of -$11.3M, a net loss of -$10.5M on $10M revenue (-105% profit margin), and relies on external financing to survive [4]. Operating cash flow of -$1.8M quarterly means the company is approximately 2-3 quarters from needing additional capital at current burn rates. The $10M market cap and sub-$1 stock price create significant dilution risk from future equity raises. No forward P/E or PEG is calculable because no analyst covers this micro-cap. Enterprise pipeline targets ($10M+ ARR from the large employer, $2M from telecom, $2-6M from cities) are aspirational and subject to negotiation failure — none are contracted. Daily liquidity of just $1.17M makes the stock difficult to trade without significant price impact, and any deterioration in volume or price could breach the IOWN liquidity floor [5].
Key Catalysts
