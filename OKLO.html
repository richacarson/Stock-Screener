Company Profile
Utilities ·Utilities - Independent Power Producers ·NYSE ·United States
Oklo Inc. develops advanced fission power plants to provide clean, reliable, affordable energy at scale. The company's Aurora Powerhouse is a liquid metal-cooled reactor designed to produce between 15 and 75 megawatts of electricity. Oklo is also commercializing nuclear fuel recycling and fuel fabrication technology that can convert used nuclear fuel into usable fuel for its reactors. Founded in 2013 and headquartered in Santa Clara, California.
