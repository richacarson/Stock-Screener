Company Profile

The OLB Group, Inc. provides integrated financial and transaction processing services for small and mid-sized merchants in the United States. The company operates through Fintech Services and Bitcoin Mining segments, offering CrowdPay crowdfunding, OmniSoft cloud business management, eVance payment processing, and SecurePay payment gateway solutions. Founded in 1993 and headquartered in New York, New York.
13 Employees ·www.olb.com
