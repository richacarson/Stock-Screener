Company Profile
Industrials ·Specialty Industrial Machinery ·NYSE American ·United States
Ocean Power Technologies designs, manufactures, and markets autonomous maritime systems and ocean intelligence solutions. The company's products include the PowerBuoy platform for offshore power generation and data collection, WAM-V autonomous surface vehicles (ASVs), and the AI-capable Merrows maritime domain awareness system. OPT serves defense, maritime security, offshore energy, and scientific research customers.
