Investment Thesis
OPT's bull case rests on its transformation into a defense-oriented autonomous maritime systems company: the $15M funded backlog (up 300% YoY), $163.9M pipeline (up 84%), DoD Secret Facility Clearance, and U.S. Navy Project Overmatch selection position it as a credible small-cap defense technology play in a market where autonomous maritime systems are a growing national security priority.
