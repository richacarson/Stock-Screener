Company Profile
Technology / Consumer Goods ·Inventory Management / Digital Identity ·NASDAQ ·United States
Eightco Holdings operates in two segments: inventory management solutions and corrugated custom packaging for North American and European markets. The company is pivoting toward digital identity and Proof of Human (PoH) verification services as an authentication layer for AI-driven environments, with its FOTAP beta testing platform scheduled for April 2026. Formerly known as Cryptyde, Inc., the company carries an extreme share dilution profile — shares outstanding increased 883% in one year.

Investment Thesis
The speculative bull case for Eightco is the FOTAP platform: if digital human verification becomes a mandatory layer in AI-saturated digital environments (financial services, gaming, e-commerce), Eightco's first-mover position could create substantial value. The company's $23M cash position against minimal debt provides short-term financial stability, and the FOTAP beta launch in April 2026 is a near-term catalyst [1].
The bear case is dominant: the stock fails IOWN's basic liquidity threshold (avg_daily_volume × price < $1M), the company has diluted shareholders by 883% in one year, the all-time stock high was $4,537 versus the current $1.00 — a 99.98% decline — and the company has never demonstrated sustained profitability. The FOTAP platform is pre-revenue with no paying customers cited. With 23 employees, executing a digital identity platform at global scale while also running a packaging business is operationally implausible [1].
