Company Profile

Old Republic International Corporation is a Chicago-based insurance holding company founded in 1923, operating through Specialty Insurance (commercial liability, workers' compensation, aviation) and Title Insurance segments. The company insures truckers, aviation companies, cyber risk, and provides title insurance for real estate transactions.
