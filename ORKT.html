Company Profile
Technology ·Software - Application ·NASDAQ ·Singapore
Orangekloud Technology is a Singapore-based micro-cap software company offering IT consultancy services and the eMOBIQ no-code rapid mobile application development platform for SMEs. The company operates through Packaged Software Solutions and No-Code Platform segments, serving food manufacturing, precision engineering, construction, retail, and energy sectors. The company recently launched eMOBIQ AI, an AI-driven no-code development tool, and announced a reverse merger LOI with Orbis Technology (VeVe).
