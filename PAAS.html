Company Profile
Basic Materials ·Gold ·NYSE ·Canada
Pan American Silver Corp. is the world's largest primary silver producer, operating mines across Chile, Peru, Brazil, Mexico, Canada, Argentina, Bolivia, and Guatemala. The company also produces gold, zinc, lead, and copper. The Silver segment includes La Colorada, Juanicipio, Cerro Moro, Huaron, and San Vicente mines. The Gold segment includes Jacobina, El Penon, Timmins, Shahuindo, Minera Florida, and Dolores mines. Founded in 1979 and headquartered in Vancouver, Canada.
