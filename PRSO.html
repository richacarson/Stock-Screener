Company Profile
Technology ·Semiconductors ·NASDAQ ·United States
Peraso Inc. is a fabless semiconductor company specializing in millimeter-wave (mmWave) wireless technology operating in the 60 GHz band. The company designs and sells chipsets, modules, software, and intellectual property for high-bandwidth wireless connectivity applications including 5G fixed wireless access, defense communications, and enterprise networking. Founded in 2009 and headquartered in San Jose, California, Peraso completed a strategic pivot in 2024-2025 from legacy memory IC products to a pure-play mmWave wireless focus.
