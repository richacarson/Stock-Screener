Investment Thesis
Plus Therapeutics represents a high-risk, high-reward speculative play on genuinely innovative CNS cancer therapeutics. REYOBIQ's Phase 1/2 data showing 87% clinical benefit rate in leptomeningeal metastases and doubled overall survival at therapeutic doses in glioblastoma is scientifically compelling [1]. With FDA clearance for pediatric brain cancer IND, three active clinical programs, and analyst price targets of $6.75 (2,600% upside), the potential reward is enormous if the science translates to regulatory approval. The $17.6M CPRIT grant and $3M DoD grant reduce dilution risk for funded programs.
The overwhelming risk is financial survival — Plus Therapeutics has only $15M from its recent equity raise with cash runway through mid-2026, and will need multiple additional capital raises before any potential commercialization [1]. Revenue of $5.2M is declining, losses were $22.4M in 2025 (72.5% worse than 2024), and the stock at $0.24 with a $43M market cap is firmly in penny stock territory subject to extreme dilution and volatility. The 4-analyst 'Strong Buy' consensus is based on speculative biotech valuation models that assume clinical success — any trial setback would be devastating to the equity [1].

  1. ReSPECT-LM dose optimization trial results in 2026 could establish pivotal trial design for REYOBIQ in leptomeningeal metastases [1]
  2. CNSide diagnostics commercial launch could generate meaningful near-term revenue to extend cash runway [1]
