Company Profile
Industrials ·Electrical Equipment & Parts ·NASDAQ ·China
Erayak Power Solution Group Inc. researches, develops, manufactures, and sells power solution products including sine wave and off-grid inverters, inverter and gasoline generators, battery and smart chargers, and custom-designed products. The company serves agricultural, industrial, recreational, and outdoor markets internationally from its base in Wenzhou, China, and is pivoting toward U.S.-centric energy solutions.

Investment Thesis
Erayak Power Solution is a micro-cap ($4M market cap) Chinese manufacturer of power inverters and generators pivoting to become a U.S.-centric branded energy solutions company. The company generated $30.3M revenue in 2024 with 49% growth, but H1 2025 saw a 26.8% revenue decline to $4.37M [1]. The strategic pivot to AI-driven energy demand (UPS-integrated platforms for edge computing) and the Nexora U.S. brand launch represent potential catalysts, with new products scheduled for Q2 2026 and a new industrial facility expected operational in H2 2026 [1][2]. The forward-looking AI power thesis is compelling at the conceptual level — growing power demand from AI infrastructure could benefit portable/backup power providers.
The bear case is substantial: this is a $4M market cap company with negative profitability (-2.1% profit margin, -2.2% ROE), declining revenue (-26.8% YoY), and only 360 employees competing against well-capitalized giants like Generac ($8B+) and Honda in the portable power market [4]. The stock has declined from $6.06 (50-day SMA) to $4.12, reflecting market skepticism. The China-to-U.S. pivot involves significant execution risk — establishing brand recognition, distribution channels, and regulatory compliance in a new market while managing a declining core business. The extreme micro-cap status means minimal analyst coverage, institutional ownership, and liquidity. The AI power narrative is early-stage with no quantified revenue. For IOWN, this stock is uninvestable at current scale despite an interesting strategic direction.
