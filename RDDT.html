Investment Thesis
Reddit is a high-growth digital advertising platform delivering exceptional financial performance — Q4 2025 revenue of $726M (+70% YoY), full-year revenue of $2.2B (+69%), and net income of $530M with 91% gross margins [1]. At 15.1x forward P/E, the stock trades at a remarkable discount for 70% revenue growth, reflecting the 50% YTD stock decline that has created a potential entry point. DAUs of 121M (+19%) and ARPU of $5.98 (+42%) demonstrate both audience growth and improving monetization. The AI-powered Max Campaigns suite lowered advertiser CPA by 17%, with active advertisers up 75% — indicating the flywheel is accelerating [1]. Free cash flow of $684M and $2.5B in cash/securities plus a $1B buyback provide substantial capital flexibility.
The bear case centers on AI cannibalization risk and revenue concentration. Reddit derives 94% of revenue from advertising, making it highly sensitive to digital ad spending cycles — competing against Meta, Google, TikTok, and Amazon for ad budgets [1]. The most existential risk is AI substitution: ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity increasingly provide direct answers to the advice-seeking queries that drive Reddit's search traffic, and Reddit's own data licensing deals train these competing AI models [2]. Q1 2026 guidance implies deceleration to ~53% growth, and comparisons toughen through the year. The stock has fallen 50% YTD despite strong earnings, suggesting the market is pricing in structural growth deceleration beyond what management guides. Content moderation risk persists — Reddit hosts explicit content and has faced scrutiny on harmful material, with an Inspire score of -88 reflecting significant social concerns [3]. Glassdoor reviews flag leadership accountability issues and frequent re-orgs that could impact execution quality [2].
Key Catalysts
  1. AI-powered Max Campaigns driving 75% advertiser growth and 17% CPA improvement, with lower-funnel conversion volume doubling YoY — indicating the ad monetization flywheel is accelerating [1]
  2. Stock down 50% YTD at 15.1x forward P/E creates potential value entry — if Reddit sustains 40%+ revenue growth through 2026, the current valuation implies significant upside rerating [1]
  3. Data licensing partnerships with Google and OpenAI monetize Reddit's unique human-generated content corpus, adding a non-advertising revenue stream with high margins [1]
Key Risks
  1. AI cannibalization — ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity increasingly answer advice-seeking queries that drive Reddit's search traffic, and Reddit's own data licensing deals train competing AI models, creating a self-inflicted disruption vector [2]
  2. Advertising concentration (94% of revenue) exposes Reddit to digital ad spending cycles, competing against Meta, Google, TikTok, and Amazon with a fraction of their scale and data advantages [1]
  3. Content moderation and platform safety — Reddit hosts explicit content contributing to an Inspire score of -88 (MISALIGNED), creating advertiser brand safety concerns and potential regulatory risk in multiple jurisdictions [3]

AI Resilience (25%)
AI Resilience MODERATE
5
AI Risk Protection (4/9): Reddit faces meaningful AI disruption risk from multiple vectors. AI chatbots (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity) increasingly provide direct answers to the advice-seeking queries that drive Reddit's search traffic — if users ask AI instead of searching Reddit, traffic and ad impressions decline [2]. Ironically, Reddit's own data licensing deals (Google, OpenAI) train the AI models that could eventually cannibalize its traffic. AI-generated content could dilute the authentic human discussions that are Reddit's core value proposition. Analysts have flagged that Reddit is 'benefitting currently from the AI bubble' with uncertainty about long-term sustainability [2]. However, Reddit's community structure, real-time discussion, and niche expertise create defensibility that AI cannot fully replicate — people seek human connection, not just answers. AI Opportunity (1/1): Reddit generates meaningful AI revenue through data licensing partnerships with Google and OpenAI, plus the AI-powered Max Campaigns ad suite that lowered CPA by 17% and drove 75% advertiser growth [1].
