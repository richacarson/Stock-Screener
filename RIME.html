Company Profile

Algorhythm Holdings (formerly The Singing Machine Company) is an AI technology holding company operating SemiCab, an AI-enabled collaborative logistics platform that uses machine learning to optimize freight routing and reduce empty truck miles. The company divested its legacy karaoke business in August 2025 to focus entirely on AI-driven logistics SaaS.

Investment Thesis
Algorhythm Holdings' SemiCab platform demonstrates genuinely impressive AI logistics capabilities — 4x workforce productivity gains, 70% reduction in empty freight miles, and 300–400% volume scaling without headcount increases [2]. The company added four Fortune 500 clients in India, achieved record ARR representing a 300% increase, and projects $15–20M annualized revenue by end of 2026 [1]. The $450B U.S. full-truckload market is massive, and SemiCab's Apex SaaS platform launched in November 2025 targets this opportunity directly. One analyst rates RIME as Strong Buy with a $5 price target. If the company can scale revenue while managing cash burn, the current $6.3M market cap could represent significant upside.
The bear case is overwhelming for institutional investment. The company has a $6.3M market cap, 25 employees, D/E of 6,811%, profit margin of -108.8%, and lost more money than it generated in revenue over the last year [4]. The stock trades at $1.10 — firmly in penny stock territory with the associated risks of dilution, delisting, and manipulation. Revenue declined 62% over three years as the karaoke business collapsed before the SemiCab pivot began generating revenue [1]. The company was acquired by Digital Power Lending, raising questions about future corporate governance and strategic direction. Larger, better-funded competitors (Uber Freight, C.H. Robinson, XPO Logistics) are deploying AI logistics solutions with vastly more resources, distribution, and customer relationships. The gap between compelling AI technology and viable investment is enormous.
