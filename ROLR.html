New CEO Seth Young (ex-PointsBet) and a new AI-focused COO have brought iGaming pedigree and a $26M January 2026 capital raise to fund the pivot [1][4]. Q4 2025 saw net income from continuing operations swing positive to $2.7M from a $3.0M loss, suggesting cost discipline is improving [1]. For speculators willing to underwrite execution risk on prediction markets and Web3, the option value is real — but this is a high-risk micro-cap, not an institutional-grade investment.
