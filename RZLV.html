Rezolve AI PLC provides generative AI solutions for the retail and e-commerce sectors. The company sells its AI-powered commerce platform — including Brain Commerce, Brain Checkout, and Brain Assistant — to retailers, brands, manufacturers, banks, and other enterprise customers. Founded in 2016, the company is headquartered in London, United Kingdom and operates in the UK and United States.

Investment Thesis
Rezolve AI is a hyper-growth AI commerce company that generated $60M in Q1 2026 revenue alone — surpassing its entire FY2025 total — and has raised FY2026 guidance to $360M with a $500M ARR exit run rate target. The platform's 90%+ core software margins and $750M+ in secured funding provide a runway to scale without additional equity dilution.
The growth trajectory is among the most aggressive in public AI companies, but the investment case requires significant caution [1][2]. The company lost $101M on $46.8M in revenue in FY2025, has -98.7% ROE, and trades at $2.81 — down 67% from its 52-week high of $8.45. The aggressive revenue guidance revisions (from $350M to $360M in weeks) and promotional press release language raise questions about revenue quality and sustainability. Competing against Salesforce, Adobe, and Shopify in AI-powered commerce with no demonstrated moat beyond early traction makes this a high-risk speculative position.
Key Catalysts
  1. Q1 2026 revenue of $60M exceeded full FY2025 revenue in just 90 days, with FY2026 guidance raised to $360M representing ~670% YoY growth [1][2]
  2. Core software margins exceeding 90% with $232M+ in contracted ARR entering 2026 provide visibility into revenue acceleration [1]
  3. Over $750M in total funding secured with no requirement for additional operational equity, enabling continued scale without dilution [1]
Key Risks
  1. Deeply unprofitable with -217% profit margin and $101M net loss on $46.8M FY2025 revenue — path to GAAP profitability is unclear despite high gross margins [5]
  2. Stock down 67% from 52-week high of $8.45 to $2.81, suggesting significant market skepticism about the sustainability of hyper-growth guidance and revenue quality
  3. Competing against dominant incumbents (Salesforce Einstein, Adobe Sensei, Shopify AI) in AI commerce with no demonstrated switching costs, network effects, or regulatory moats — existential competitive risk for a $1.1B company [3]
