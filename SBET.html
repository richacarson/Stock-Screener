Company Profile
Financial Services ·Capital Markets ·NASDAQ ·United States
SharpLink, Inc. (formerly SharpLink Gaming) is a Nasdaq-listed company operating as an institutional-grade Ethereum treasury platform and sports betting affiliate marketing technology provider. The company holds approximately 868,699 ETH (~$1.72B) and provides AI-powered personalization tools (BetSense) for sportsbook operators. SharpLink rebranded in February 2026 to reflect its dual focus on digital asset treasury management and sports technology.

Investment Thesis
SharpLink offers a unique dual exposure to Ethereum appreciation and AI-powered sports betting technology. The company holds 868,699 ETH (~$1.72B), making it one of the largest public Ethereum holders, with 13,615 ETH in staking rewards generated since June 2025 [1]. The $425M Consensys-led investment and Joseph Lubin (Ethereum co-founder) as Board Chairman provide institutional credibility. Revenue surged 660% to $28.1M in 2025, institutional ownership reached 46%, and the forward P/E of 3.04x suggests analysts expect meaningful profitability [1]. The BetSense AI personalization platform, licensed in 21 US states covering 98% of the North American sports betting market, provides a technology revenue stream independent of crypto volatility [2].
The bear case is substantial. SharpLink posted a $734.6M net loss in 2025, driven by unrealized ETH impairments, demonstrating the extreme volatility of the treasury strategy [1]. The $3.2B in capital raised to buy Ethereum represents massive shareholder dilution, and the entire investment thesis depends on ETH price appreciation — if Ethereum declines significantly, the company's asset base and equity value collapse. The sports betting affiliate marketing business generated declining revenue before the crypto pivot, raising questions about the core business viability. ROE is -60.7% and trailing profit margin is deeply negative [3]. The corporate identity crisis — sports betting company → crypto treasury → rebranded as 'Sharplink, Inc.' — suggests a company without strategic conviction. No ISS governance ratings exist, and the co-CEO structure with frequent leadership changes adds governance risk. The entire value proposition is essentially a leveraged ETH ETF with a sports betting sidecar.
Key Catalysts
  1. Ethereum price appreciation driving mark-to-market gains on 868,699 ETH holdings (~$1.72B at current prices), with staking rewards compounding at 13,615 ETH since June 2025 [1]
  2. BetSense AI personalization platform expansion across 21 licensed US states, with the C4 generative AI recommendation engine potentially increasing sportsbook conversion rates [2]
  3. Growing institutional ownership (46% as of Q4 2025, with ~60 new institutions added in Q4) validating the ETH treasury model and potentially driving share price appreciation [1]
Key Risks
  1. Ethereum price collapse risk — the company's $1.72B ETH position is subject to extreme cryptocurrency volatility, and a repeat of the 2022 crypto winter could trigger catastrophic losses [1]
  2. Massive shareholder dilution — $3.2B in capital raised to fund ETH purchases has dramatically diluted existing shareholders, with ETH per share of 4.01 requiring sustained ETH appreciation to justify [1]
  3. Strategic identity crisis — the pivot from sports betting to ETH treasury obscures the company's core competency, with no clear path to sustainable operating profitability independent of crypto market conditions [2]
