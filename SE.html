Investment Thesis
Sea Limited is a rare emerging-market internet platform that has achieved profitability while sustaining hyper-growth. Full-year 2025 revenue hit $22.9B (+36% YoY) with $1.6B net income (+260% YoY), and management guided 25% Shopee GMV growth for 2026 with plans to double fulfillment penetration [1]. The trifecta of Shopee (e-commerce leader in Southeast Asia with $127B GMV), SeaMoney (60% revenue growth, expanding into banking and insurance), and Garena (100M DAU, double-digit bookings growth guided) creates a self-reinforcing flywheel with a $10.4B cash cushion that provides substantial runway for continued investment [1].
The bear case centers on margin sustainability and competition. Shopee's cost of revenue grew 43.2% against 35.8% revenue growth in Q4 2025, driven by logistics scaling costs, and Q4 EPS of $0.63 missed the $0.80 consensus by 21% [1]. TikTok Shop is aggressively expanding in Southeast Asia, and Lazada (backed by Alibaba) remains a well-funded competitor. Three major brokers — Bernstein, Benchmark, and Jefferies — cut price targets by $20-57 following Q4 results [1]. At a forward P/E of 15.3 the valuation is reasonable, but the path from 6.9% profit margin to management's 2-3% e-commerce EBITDA margin target requires sustained execution in a competitive and price-sensitive market.
Key Catalysts
  1. Doubling of Shopee fulfillment order penetration by end-2026, which should improve delivery speed, customer retention, and unit economics across Southeast Asia's fragmented logistics landscape
  2. SeaMoney's expansion from credit lending into digital banking and insurance, with licenses already secured in multiple markets and a $5.1B loan book providing the foundation for cross-selling financial products
  3. Garena's double-digit bookings growth guidance for 2026, driven by new IP collaborations and football-related promotions that could reignite engagement in the gaming segment
Key Risks
  1. TikTok Shop's aggressive content-commerce push in Southeast Asia threatens Shopee's market share lead, particularly among younger demographics who increasingly discover and purchase products through short-form video
  2. Margin compression risk as logistics costs scale faster than revenue — Shopee's cost of revenue grew 43.2% vs 35.8% revenue growth in Q4 2025, and the path to management's 2-3% EBITDA margin target remains uncertain
  3. Regulatory risk across multiple emerging-market jurisdictions where SeaMoney operates digital banking and lending services, particularly as governments tighten fintech oversight and capital requirements
