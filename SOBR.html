Company Profile
Technology ·Scientific & Technical Instruments ·NASDAQ ·United States
SOBR Safe is a Denver-based company founded in 2020 that develops transdermal, touch-based alcohol detection technology for workplace safety and wellness. Flagship products include SOBRcheck, a touch-based non-invasive alcohol detection device that detects alcohol vapor emitted through the skin, and SOBRsure, a wearable wristband for continuous passive alcohol monitoring [1].
