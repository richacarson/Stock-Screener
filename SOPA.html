Company Profile
Technology ·Internet Content & Information ·NASDAQ ·Singapore
Society Pass Incorporated is a Singapore-headquartered technology company that operates digital media and e-commerce platforms across Southeast Asia, with operations in Vietnam, Indonesia, the Philippines, and Thailand. The company has pivoted multiple times — from a loyalty rewards platform to e-commerce marketplace to travel booking (NusaTrip) to an announced AI-driven data center strategy in late 2025. CEO Raynauld Liang succeeded founder Dennis Nguyen. Society Pass filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection on May 12, 2026, and received a Nasdaq delisting notice. At the time of filing, the company had approximately $7.1M in trailing twelve-month revenue and a market capitalization below $5M.
