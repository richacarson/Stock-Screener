Investment Thesis
SS&C Technologies offers compelling value at 9.9x forward adjusted P/E for a recurring-revenue software business with record 2025 results, 5% organic growth, and 2026 adjusted EPS guidance of $6.70-$7.02 (~12% growth). Founder-CEO Bill Stone's 40-year track record of disciplined acquisitions (most recently Calastone for $1.03 billion) has built a 150+ product platform with high switching costs. The agentic AI strategy positions SS&C at the forefront of financial operations automation. Operating cash flow of $1.74 billion (up 25.7%) with cash conversion above 100% and targeted EBITDA margin expansion to 40% demonstrate improving profitability.
The 111% debt-to-equity ratio reflects SS&C's acquisition-heavy strategy, creating leverage risk in a slowing economy. GAAP EPS declined 22.1% in 2025 (due to acquisition-related amortization and one-time charges), highlighting the gap between GAAP and adjusted earnings that investors must navigate. The 2025 whistleblower lawsuit alleging retaliation against employees raises governance concerns. Integration risk from the $1.03 billion Calastone acquisition could create operational distraction. The financial services software market is highly competitive with FIS, Broadridge, and fintech startups. No single client exceeds 5% of revenue, which provides diversification but also limits pricing power.
