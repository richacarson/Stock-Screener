Company Profile
Energy ·Oil & Gas Equipment & Services ·NASDAQ ·China
STAK Inc. is a Changzhou, China-based company specializing in the research, development, manufacturing, and sale of oilfield-specialized production and maintenance equipment including oil pumping trucks, well repair trucks, fracking trucks, and automation control modules. The company IPO'd on Nasdaq in February 2025 and operates primarily in China's domestic oilfield services market.

Investment Thesis
STAK offers exposure to China's oilfield services recovery with strong revenue momentum — H1 FY2025 revenue grew 24% to $17.0M driven by 73% growth in equipment sales, with gross margins improving to 30.7% [1]. The company holds 79 patents and copyrights in specialized oilfield automation, was recognized by Jiangsu Province for innovation, and is expanding its product portfolio toward higher-margin specialized equipment. At 0.77x price-to-sales and 1.02x price-to-book, the valuation is undemanding if the growth trajectory continues. The $19.2M market cap offers significant upside potential if the company achieves profitability and international expansion [1].
The bear case is severe for this micro-cap. STAK has a $19.2M market cap, only $1.0M cash, negative free cash flow of -$3.7M, and a quick ratio of 0.23, creating real going-concern risk [1]. The stock has collapsed 75% from its $4.00 IPO price, received a Nasdaq minimum bid price compliance notice, and completed a deeply dilutive offering at $0.38 per unit. The Cayman Islands holding structure with operations entirely in China introduces significant VIE/regulatory risk. With only 47 employees and $24.9M revenue, the company is extremely small-scale and dependent on China's domestic oilfield market. Short interest at 66% of float is extraordinarily high, reflecting intense bearish conviction. No analyst coverage exists for this name [1].
