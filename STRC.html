Company Profile
Technology ·Software - Application ·NASDAQ ·United States
Strategy Inc. (formerly MicroStrategy) operates as a Bitcoin treasury company and enterprise analytics software provider. The company holds 815,061 BTC as its primary treasury reserve asset and offers the Strategy ONE AI-powered business intelligence platform. STRC is the company's variable rate preferred security, providing investors fixed-income exposure to the Strategy Inc. capital structure with a current yield of approximately 11.55%.

Investment Thesis
STRC is Strategy Inc.'s variable rate preferred security offering approximately 11.55% yield backed by the capital structure of the world's largest corporate Bitcoin holder (815,061 BTC) [1]. The preferred provides income exposure to Strategy's Bitcoin-leveraged balance sheet with priority over common equity. The underlying company raised $25.3B in 2025 to fund Bitcoin accumulation, and Subscription Services in the legacy BI platform grew 62% YoY to $123M quarterly [1]. CEO Phong Le and multiple insiders have been actively purchasing shares, with $4.2M+ in insider buying over the past 12 months [5]. The stock trades near par (~$99.53) with robust daily liquidity of $248M.
The bear case is dominated by extraordinary leverage and concentration risk. Strategy's D/E ratio of 1,615.8% reflects $25.3B+ in capital raised primarily through debt and equity to buy Bitcoin — a single, volatile asset with 50%+ drawdown history [1][5]. A severe Bitcoin bear market could trigger a liquidity crisis that threatens the preferred's dividend payments and principal. The software business (revenue flat at $477M with -1.5% 3-year CAGR) is being neglected in favor of Bitcoin, and Glassdoor ratings of 2.8/5 suggest organizational decay [4]. The company lost $3.85B in FY2025 on a GAAP basis. STRC holders face subordination risk if additional debt layers are added to fund Bitcoin purchases. The entire investment thesis rests on Bitcoin's long-term appreciation — if Bitcoin stagnates or declines materially, the leveraged structure could impair the preferred's recovery value. ESG concerns around Bitcoin's energy consumption add reputational risk [6].
Key Catalysts
  1. Bitcoin price appreciation driving asset value growth — each 10% Bitcoin increase adds roughly $7B+ to Strategy's treasury value, supporting the capital structure backing STRC [1]
  2. Subscription Services 62% YoY growth demonstrating the Strategy ONE cloud/AI platform's competitive viability and providing a revenue floor independent of Bitcoin [1]
  3. Continued insider buying by CEO Le and directors signals management confidence in the capital structure's sustainability [5]
Key Risks
  1. Extreme leverage (D/E 1,615.8%) concentrated in a single volatile asset — a 50%+ Bitcoin drawdown (which has occurred multiple times historically) could threaten STRC dividend payments and principal value [1][5]
  2. Organizational culture deterioration with 2.8/5 Glassdoor rating and reports of toxic leadership and innovation suppression, threatening the viability of the software business that provides non-Bitcoin revenue [4]
  3. Regulatory risk — potential government restrictions on corporate Bitcoin holdings, cryptocurrency taxation changes, or SEC scrutiny of the leveraged treasury strategy could fundamentally alter the company's business model [6]
