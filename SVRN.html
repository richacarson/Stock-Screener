Company Profile
Industrials ·Marine Shipping ·NASDAQ ·Greece
OceanPal Inc. operates a dual business model: a legacy dry bulk shipping fleet of three Panamax carriers and one MR2 product tanker, and SovereignAI Services LLC, a digital asset treasury subsidiary holding approximately 51.3 million NEAR Protocol tokens ($77.5M) with plans to build confidential AI cloud infrastructure. The company changed its ticker from OP to SVRN in November 2025.

Investment Thesis
OceanPal offers a unique public market vehicle for NEAR Protocol exposure through SovereignAI, holding 51.3M NEAR tokens worth $77.5M against a $12M market cap, creating a significant NAV discount [1]. Stockholders' equity of $156.6M versus $12M market cap represents a potential 13x upside to book value. The NEAR staking yield generates approximately $3.6M in annualized revenue, and the advisory board (NEAR co-founder Illia Polosukhin, OpenAI researcher Lukasz Kaiser) lends credibility to the AI infrastructure vision [2]. If NEAR token price appreciates and the AI cloud platform materializes, the upside is asymmetric. The shipping segment provides $1.5-2.5M in EBITDA stability [1].
The bear case is severe. OceanPal has destroyed 99%+ of shareholder value since its 2021 IPO, with the stock falling from an all-time high of $60,450 (split-adjusted) to $7.10 [3]. The $120M PIPE massively diluted existing shareholders to fund a speculative crypto treasury strategy with no proven business model. Revenue declined 37.4% to $13.4M with a $65.2M net loss in 2025 [1]. The NEAR token position creates concentrated crypto risk — a 50% decline in NEAR would erase most of the balance sheet value. Nasdaq delisting risk required a 1-for-20 reverse split just to maintain listing compliance [1]. The company has no operational AI products, no proven technology team, and no revenue from AI infrastructure. The dual shipping/crypto model lacks strategic coherence, and the SovereignAI advisory board's participation does not guarantee execution. This is a speculative vehicle, not an operating business.
Key Catalysts
  1. NEAR Protocol token price appreciation — SovereignAI holds 4.2% of total NEAR supply with plans to reach 10%, creating leveraged upside to NEAR token performance [1][2]
  2. AI cloud platform development with NEAR Foundation and NVIDIA technologies could create real infrastructure revenue beyond staking yields [2]
  3. Shipping segment stabilization with $13-14M revenue guidance and $1.5-2.5M EBITDA in 2026, no drydockings scheduled [1]
Key Risks
  1. Concentrated crypto exposure — 51.3M NEAR tokens represent the majority of balance sheet value, and a sustained NEAR price decline would trigger significant losses and potential equity impairment [1]
  2. Nasdaq delisting risk — even after the 1-for-20 reverse split, maintaining $1.00 minimum bid price requires sustained stock appreciation from current levels [1]
  3. Extreme dilution and value destruction — 99%+ shareholder loss since 2021 IPO with continued dilution through PIPE transactions, making this a highly speculative vehicle with no proven path to operational profitability [1][3]
