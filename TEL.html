Investment Thesis
TE Connectivity is a premier AI infrastructure beneficiary delivering accelerating growth: Q1 FY2026 revenue grew 22% with record orders of $5.1 billion (up 28%) and adjusted operating margins expanding 180bps to 22.2%. The company's high-speed connectors, liquid cooling, and power solutions are critical enablers of AI data center scaling where demand is 'essentially insatiable.' The Industrial segment's 24% FY2025 growth driven by AI and energy customers demonstrates strong secular tailwinds. At 15.8x forward earnings with a clean 46% D/E balance sheet, $1.99 billion free cash flow, and a 13-year dividend growth streak, TEL offers compelling value for AI-exposed industrial technology.
The negative Inspire score of -49 due to LGBT Activism creates headwinds for faith-based investors. The Transportation segment faces cyclical auto production risk, and content growth from EV electrification could slow if EV adoption decelerates. Revenue growth of 22% in Q1 FY2026 creates a tough comparable base, and guidance for Q2 FY2026 of 13% reported growth already shows deceleration. The company's Ireland domicile introduces geopolitical and tax policy risk. Automotive exposure (roughly half of revenue) makes TE vulnerable to global auto production downturns, and the US-China trade tensions could impact the company's ~140-country operations.
