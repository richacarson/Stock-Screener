Company Profile
Healthcare ·Medical Devices / Biopharmaceuticals ·NASDAQ ·United States
Tivic Health Systems (rebranding to Valion Bio in April 2026) is a late-stage immunotherapeutics and bioelectronic medicine company. After winding down its ClearUP consumer bioelectronic device business in October 2025, the company pivoted to developing Entolimod, a TLR5 agonist in late-stage development for acute radiation syndrome and neutropenia, along with CDMO services. Formerly focused on vagus nerve stimulation for sinus pain relief.
