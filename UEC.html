Company Profile
Energy ·Uranium ·AMEX ·United States
Uranium Energy Corp. is America's largest and fastest-growing uranium supplier, advancing low-cost, environmentally friendly in-situ recovery (ISR) mining projects in Wyoming and South Texas, plus high-grade conventional projects in Canada. The company operates hub-and-spoke ISR platforms with a near debt-free balance sheet and maintains a physical uranium inventory of 1.46 million pounds.
171 Employees ·www.uraniumenergy.com

Investment Thesis
UEC offers pure-play exposure to the nuclear energy renaissance driven by AI data center demand, U.S. energy security imperatives, and the global pledge to triple nuclear capacity by 2050 [2]. The company's near debt-free balance sheet with $488M in cash, 1.46M lb physical uranium inventory, and multiple projects ramping production (Christensen Ranch, Burke Hollow, Ludeman, Sweetwater, Roughrider) create a diversified platform [1]. Analyst forecasts project 123% annual earnings growth and 44.5% annual revenue growth as production scales. The Inspire Insight score of 33 (ALIGNED) with 11 positive ESG attributions — including Environmental Risk Mitigation and Low Ecological Impact — reflects strong sustainability practices for a mining company [5].
The bear case rests on execution and valuation. Revenue declined from $66.8M to $20.2M for the nine months ended April 2026, and the Q3 net loss widened to $52.3M from $30.2M a year prior [1]. The $5.9B market cap on minimal revenue creates extreme valuation risk — any production shortfall or uranium price decline could trigger significant compression. The company raised capital through ATM issuances and a public offering, diluting shareholders to fund expansion. Regulatory permitting delays from NRC backlogs could extend production timelines beyond expectations. The UR&C refining and conversion ambitions remain pre-feasibility with no guaranteed timeline. UEC is a single-commodity bet on uranium demand, with no hedge against a nuclear buildout slowdown or shift to alternative energy technologies [4].
