Company Profile
Technology ·Semiconductors ·NYSE ·Taiwan
United Microelectronics Corporation is a global semiconductor wafer foundry operating in Taiwan, China, Japan, and Singapore. The company manufactures and markets integrated circuits for applications in computing, communications, consumer electronics, and automotive. UMC focuses on mature and specialty process nodes from 14nm to 0.5 micron, with its 22nm platform representing the primary growth driver and AI-related revenue expected to grow to $3 billion.
