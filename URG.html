Ur-Energy is a U.S. uranium mining company operating the Lost Creek in-situ recovery (ISR) facility in Wyoming and commissioning the Shirley Basin ISR project (initial operations commenced April 2026). The company holds 31.4 million pounds of combined uranium resources and has multi-year delivery commitments across eight sales agreements.
