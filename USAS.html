Company Profile
Basic Materials ·Silver ·NYSE ·Canada
Americas Gold and Silver Corporation engages in the exploration, development, and production of precious and base metals in the Americas. The company operates the Galena Complex in Idaho (high-grade silver, lead, copper) and the Cosala Operations in Mexico (silver, zinc, lead). Recently expanded into antimony processing through a JV with United States Antimony Corporation. Incorporated in 1998, headquartered in Toronto, Canada.

Investment Thesis
Americas Gold and Silver is executing a dramatic turnaround under CEO Paul Andre Huet, swinging to a $10M Q1 2026 profit on $67.8M revenue (up 187% YoY) from a $19.7M loss in the year-ago quarter, driven by record silver production of 787,000 ounces and four new high-grade discoveries at the Galena Complex [1]. The company holds $122.4M in cash, maintains a clean balance sheet (D/E ~25%), and has guided for approximately 30% production growth in 2026. The antimony processing JV addresses a U.S.-critical mineral with strategic national security importance, adding a differentiated revenue stream. With silver prices elevated and Huet's track record of successful mining turnarounds (Karora, Klondex), the operational trajectory is compelling [2].
The bear case centers on execution risk and structural challenges. TTM financials remain deeply negative (net income -$57.8M, ROE -39.3%, profit margin -35.6%) as the Q1 2026 turnaround has not yet annualized, and one profitable quarter does not guarantee sustained profitability [1]. The Cosala Operations in Mexico have a history of operational disruptions including the 2020 illegal blockade, and any recurrence would impair production targets [3]. Silver price volatility is a significant risk — the company's margins are highly sensitive to precious metals prices, and a silver price decline below $25/oz could erase profitability entirely. CEO Huet's track record of building mining companies for sale (Klondex to Hecla 2018, Karora to Westgold 2024) raises questions about whether the current strategy is optimized for long-term shareholders or for a near-term exit. At $2.34B market cap, the stock is pricing in significant future production growth that has yet to materialize.

AI Risk Protection (8/9): Underground silver and gold mining is one of the most AI-immune business models. Mining operations face geological uncertainty, environmental compliance, and safety requirements that are inherently human-intensive. AI may actually increase demand for silver through electronics, solar panels, and AI data center infrastructure (silver is used in electrical contacts and connections).
