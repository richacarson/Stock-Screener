VolitionRx Limited is a multi-national epigenetics company developing and commercializing blood-based diagnostic tests using its proprietary Nu.Q platform technology. The company's tests detect epigenetic changes in nucleosomes for early diagnosis of cancer, sepsis, and other diseases. Operates through subsidiaries in Belgium, the United States, the United Kingdom, and Singapore.
