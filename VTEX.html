Investment Thesis
VTEX is a well-positioned digital commerce platform making an aggressive pivot to AI-native architecture, with an Inspire score of 47 (ALIGNED) reflecting strong ethical practices across 10 positive categories. The forward PE of 14.7x is compelling for a profitable SaaS company targeting low-twenties operating margins, and the AI commerce suite launched April 2026 could accelerate growth.
However, growth has decelerated materially (subscription revenue +7.9% in FY2025 vs. higher historical rates), the $668M market cap creates small-cap volatility risk, and heavy LatAm exposure introduces persistent FX headwinds. The trailing PE of 35.6x looks expensive relative to current growth, though the forward PE suggests earnings expansion is priced in. The stock needs to demonstrate that AI-native capabilities translate to reaccelerated customer acquisition and GMV growth.
