Company Profile
Financial Services ·Asset Management ·NASDAQ ·United States
WhiteHorse Finance, Inc. is an externally managed, non-diversified, closed-end management investment company that has elected to be treated as a business development company (BDC). The company specializes in originating senior secured loans to lower middle market companies with enterprise values between $50 million and $350 million, having invested $2.91 billion across 275 transactions since its December 2012 IPO.
