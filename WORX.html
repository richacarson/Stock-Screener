Investment Thesis
SCWorx is a micro-cap healthcare data company with a legitimate AI-driven supply chain management platform but catastrophic execution history. The 2020 COVID-19 test kit fraud resulted in SEC enforcement, criminal conviction of the former CEO, and permanent reputational damage. Despite new leadership under Tim Hannibal and a narrowing net loss in Q1 2026 ($174K vs $476K prior year), the company faces Nasdaq delisting, sub-$400K market cap, and severe illiquidity.
The healthcare supply chain data market is genuinely large and underserved, and SCWorx's AI-powered item master technology addresses a real pain point. However, the company lacks the capital, scale, and credibility to compete against well-funded healthcare IT incumbents. The liquidity floor failure and delisting proceedings make this stock uninvestable for any institutional or rules-based portfolio.
